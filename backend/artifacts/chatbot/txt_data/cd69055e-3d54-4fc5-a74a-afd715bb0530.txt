cd69055e-3d54-4fc5-a74a-afd715bb0530: The Dr Lena Cansdale Scholarship is a field-specific scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences in Australia. It provides $3,000 in funding for undergraduate students majoring in Hebrew, Biblical and Jewish Studies who have completed specific units at the university. Eligible candidates must maintain a distinction average in relevant units and have completed at least 2 units in one semester or 4 units within a year. The application deadline is March 7, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/the-dr-lena-cansdale-scholarship.html.
