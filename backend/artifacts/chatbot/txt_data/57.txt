57: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is offered by Imperial College London in the United Kingdom. This scholarship is for PhD candidates in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. It provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and demonstrate English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is also required. The application deadline is September 14, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
