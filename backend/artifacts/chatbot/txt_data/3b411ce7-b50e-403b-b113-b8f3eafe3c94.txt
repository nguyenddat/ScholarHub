3b411ce7-b50e-403b-b113-b8f3eafe3c94: The University of Leeds offers PhD Scholarships in the School of Computer Science for international students. These scholarships provide partial funding covering tuition fees for PhD studies in Computer Science and Engineering in the United Kingdom. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields and submit a research proposal aligned with department goals. GRE scores may be required for some, and candidates should have at least one peer-reviewed publication. The application deadline is July 30, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
