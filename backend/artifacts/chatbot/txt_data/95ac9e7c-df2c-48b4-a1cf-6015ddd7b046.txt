95ac9e7c-df2c-48b4-a1cf-6015ddd7b046: The Kerkyasharian and Kayikian Fund for Armenian Studies is a scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences in Australia. It is available for Honours, Master's by Research, or PhD students and academic affiliates conducting research in 19th century or earlier Armenian studies. The funding amount typically ranges from $4,000 to $10,000. Applicants must be enrolled in the relevant degree program at the time of application, and selection is based on academic excellence, merit, and the quality of the research project. The application deadline is June 5, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/kerkyasharian-kayikian-fund-armenian-studies.html.
