9af986e0-9203-47d1-828e-0bf767fc93df: The University of Glasgow offers a PhD scholarship in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing, focusing on Applied and Pure Sciences and Computer Science. This partial funding scholarship covers tuition fees and is available for full-time PhD studies in the UK. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and have published at least one peer-reviewed paper. The scholarship emphasizes a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research. The application deadline is September 12, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
