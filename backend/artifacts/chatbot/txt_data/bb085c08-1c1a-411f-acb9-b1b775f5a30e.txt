bb085c08-1c1a-411f-acb9-b1b775f5a30e: The Ernest Heine Family Foundation Digital Music Scholarship is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Music. Located in Australia, it supports students enrolled in the Digital Music and Media Program at the Sydney Conservatorium of Music. The scholarship provides funding of $20,000 per annum for up to four years. Applicants must have an unconditional offer for a Bachelor of Music at the Conservatorium. There are no specified requirements for work experience, research, certifications, or achievements. The application deadline is not provided. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/a/ernest-heine-family-foundation-digital-music-scholarship.html.
