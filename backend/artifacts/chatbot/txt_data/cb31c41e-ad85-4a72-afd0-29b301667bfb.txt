cb31c41e-ad85-4a72-afd0-29b301667bfb: The International Entrance Award is a non-renewable scholarship offered by the University of Prince Edward Island in Canada for both undergraduate and postgraduate students. It covers tuition fees and is available to applicants from all fields of study globally. The award is reviewed upon admission, and selection is based on various criteria including education, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with specific weights assigned to each. The application deadline can be found on the official website. For more details, visit the [official link](https://www.upei.ca/scholarships-and-awards/display?awardid=463).
