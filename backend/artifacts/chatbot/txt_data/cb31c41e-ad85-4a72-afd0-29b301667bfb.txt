cb31c41e-ad85-4a72-afd0-29b301667bfb: The International Entrance Award is a non-renewable scholarship offered by the University of Prince Edward Island in Canada. It is available for both undergraduate and postgraduate students across all fields of study, covering tuition fees. The award is reviewed upon admission and selected by the Scholarships and Awards Committee. The selection criteria include education, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with specific weights assigned to each category. The application deadline can be found on the official website. For more details, visit the [official link](https://www.upei.ca/scholarships-and-awards/display?awardid=463).
