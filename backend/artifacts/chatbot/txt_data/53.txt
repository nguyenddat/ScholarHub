53: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Birmingham in the United Kingdom for international students pursuing a PhD in Engineering. The scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses, with tuition coverage included. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience and a strong interest in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards or distinctions are preferred. The application deadline is August 19, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
