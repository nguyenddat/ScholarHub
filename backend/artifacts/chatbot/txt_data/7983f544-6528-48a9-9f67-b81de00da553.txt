7983f544-6528-48a9-9f67-b81de00da553: The Architecture, Design and Planning Dean's International Merit Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for international students pursuing undergraduate degrees in Architecture and Design. The scholarship provides $1,000 in funding and is automatically considered upon application. Eligible candidates must have an ATAR of 95 or equivalent and must not have completed any prior tertiary studies. The scholarship is available for specific degree programs including Bachelor of Architecture and Environments and Bachelor of Design in Architecture. The application deadline is not specified. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/deans-international-merit-scholarship.html.
