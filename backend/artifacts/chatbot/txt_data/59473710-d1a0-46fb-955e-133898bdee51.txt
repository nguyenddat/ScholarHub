59473710-d1a0-46fb-955e-133898bdee51: The Gorilla International Scholarship is a fee waiver/discount offered by Pittsburg State University in the United States for international students pursuing undergraduate or graduate degrees in all majors. The scholarship provides funding of up to USD $6,000, renewable for up to four years for undergraduates and two years for graduates. Selection criteria include a GPA of 2.7-4.0, with corresponding funding amounts based on academic performance, as well as professional experience, scholarly activities, GRE/GMAT scores, and academic achievements. The application deadline can be found on the official website. For more details, visit [View details](https://www.pittstate.edu/admission/scholarships-financial-assistance.html).
