59473710-d1a0-46fb-955e-133898bdee51: The Gorilla International Scholarship is a fee waiver/discount offered by Pittsburg State University in the United States for both undergraduate and graduate students. It provides funding of up to USD $6,000, renewable for up to four years for undergraduates and two years for graduates. The scholarship is available to new international students across all majors. Selection criteria include a GPA of 3.8-4.0 for the highest award, with lower amounts for GPAs down to 2.7, as well as professional experience, scholarly activities, GRE/GMAT scores, and academic achievements. The application deadline can be found on the official website. For more details, visit [View details](https://www.pittstate.edu/admission/scholarships-financial-assistance.html).
