d13528f3-2f1d-43e8-9f59-79f26c914e35: The University of Otago Coursework Master's Scholarship offers a fee waiver of NZ$10,000 for coursework-based Master's students at the University of Otago, New Zealand, excluding those enrolled in the Master of Business Administration. The scholarship is open to all genders and covers all fields of study. Selection is based on academic excellence, with additional consideration for research papers valued at less than 90 points. The scholarship emphasizes achievements in academic excellence. Interested applicants should contact the university for the application deadline and further details. More information can be found on the official website: https://www.otago.ac.nz/study/scholarships/university-of-otago-coursework-masters-scholarship.
