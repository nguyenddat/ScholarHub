97f1fe22-ac23-4d39-9e94-dc99350e585c: The University of Birmingham offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a bursary. These scholarships are available to students from the EU, UK, and internationally, for full-time PhD studies in Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. All accepted PhD applications are automatically considered for these scholarships. The selection criteria include making a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. The scholarships are open to all genders and mature students. For more details on experience, certification, and achievements requirements, as well as the application deadline, please refer to the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
