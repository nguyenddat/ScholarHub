39: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity for PhD studies in Engineering at Imperial College London, UK. It covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. The scholarship is open to all international students, including those from the UK and EU, with a deadline for applications on October 30, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, at least one year of research experience, a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research, potential GRE score requirements, and demonstrated academic excellence or innovation.
