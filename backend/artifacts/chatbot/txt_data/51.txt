51: The University of Manchester offers a PhD scholarship in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing, available to students from the UK and EU. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science and Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred, and GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is August 19, 2025.
