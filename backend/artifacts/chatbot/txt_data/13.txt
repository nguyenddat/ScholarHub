13: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity provided by Imperial College London for international students, including those from the EU and the UK. It supports PhD studies in Computer Science and Engineering, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred. The application deadline is July 21, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
