6e7e4b27-c9f4-4676-9834-38dea85326b6: The Cerebral Palsy Alliance Professor Henry J Cowan Memorial Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for allied health students in Australia. It provides $1,500 in funding for undergraduate, postgraduate coursework, and postgraduate research students majoring in occupational therapy, speech pathology, or physiotherapy. Applicants must have a minimum Semester Average Mark of 50 and be enrolled or have an unconditional offer at the University of Sydney. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is September 30, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/bernard-lee.html.
