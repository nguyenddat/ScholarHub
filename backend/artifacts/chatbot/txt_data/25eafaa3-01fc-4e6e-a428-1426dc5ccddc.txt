25eafaa3-01fc-4e6e-a428-1426dc5ccddc: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Cambridge for PhD studies in the United Kingdom. These scholarships are partially funded, covering tuition fees, and are available to students from the EU, UK, and internationally. They are aimed at candidates pursuing degrees in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, with a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. Experience with large-scale software projects and a research proposal aligned with department goals are required. The application deadline is August 20, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
