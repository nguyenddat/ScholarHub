21: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a partial funding opportunity for PhD students at University College London, UK, covering tuition fees. It is open to all international students pursuing degrees in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must have experience with large-scale software projects, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred. The application deadline is August 4, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
