5f4c7340-244b-46c6-b176-2c6029989ad9: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Glasgow in the UK for PhD studies in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. These scholarships provide a stipend to support living expenses. Eligible candidates must pursue full-time studies and have experience with large-scale software projects. An interest in interdisciplinary projects is preferred. Applicants must demonstrate English proficiency with an IELTS score of 6.5 or TOEFL score of 90 and hold a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. The application deadline is November 8, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
