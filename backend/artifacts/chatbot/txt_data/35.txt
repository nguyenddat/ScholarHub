35: The University of Leeds offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to all international students in the UK. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Academic excellence or innovation is also required. The application deadline is August 21, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
