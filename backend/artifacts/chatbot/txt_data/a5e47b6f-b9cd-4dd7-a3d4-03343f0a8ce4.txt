a5e47b6f-b9cd-4dd7-a3d4-03343f0a8ce4: The Appen Inclusive AI Scholarship in Linguistics is a field-specific scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences. It is available for undergraduate students majoring in Linguistics and provides funding of $8,000 per annum for up to five years. Applicants must have a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 75 and must be either admitted or currently enrolled full-time in an undergraduate program within the Faculty. The application deadline is to be confirmed. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/appen-inclusive-ai-scholarship-in-linguistics.html).
