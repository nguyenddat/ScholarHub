8e480836-7b50-4b9d-beb2-35d35e40b73d: The UC Doctoral Scholarship is a research-based scholarship offered by UC in New Zealand for PhD students globally. It provides funding up to 28,000 NZD, equivalent to domestic tuition fees, upon achieving 120 points in thesis registration. The scholarship does not specify eligible majors, personal or academic criteria, or an application deadline. Selection criteria weights are provided for education, personal attributes, experience, research, certification, and achievements, but specific requirements are not detailed.
