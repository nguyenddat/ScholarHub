9505973b-9394-406f-873d-0ebbbcc64c60: The Học bổng Quốc tế is a partial scholarship offered by Loughborough University in the United Kingdom for undergraduate students from various majors. It provides up to a 25% reduction in tuition fees for the first year. The scholarship is available to international students, who are automatically considered for it. Additional support funds are available for musicians and sports talents, with musicians eligible for up to 500 GBP for music tuition fees and sports talents eligible for up to 5,000 GBP per year for training support if they achieve at least a basic international level in their sport. The selection criteria include education, personal attributes, experience, research, certification, and achievements.
