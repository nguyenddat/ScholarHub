50: The University of Leeds offers partial funding PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science for EU and international students. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards are considered advantageous. The application deadline is August 3, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
