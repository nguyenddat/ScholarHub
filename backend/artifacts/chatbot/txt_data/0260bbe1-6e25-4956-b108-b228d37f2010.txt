0260bbe1-6e25-4956-b108-b228d37f2010: The Roehampton Merit-based Scholarships are offered by Roehampton University in the United Kingdom for both undergraduate and postgraduate students from around the world. These scholarships, ranging from £1,000 to £4,000 over three years, are awarded based on academic excellence, musical or sports talent, and esports achievements. The scholarships cover various fields of study. Applicants are encouraged to check the official website for specific details on experience, research, and certification requirements, as well as the application deadline.
