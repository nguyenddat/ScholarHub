34: The University of Glasgow offers a partial funding PhD Studentship in Natural Language Processing for international students, including those from the EU and the UK. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend for candidates pursuing degrees in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences, or Computer Science. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Additionally, having at least one published peer-reviewed paper is preferred. The application deadline is October 19, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
