56: The University of Manchester offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, focusing on Applied and Pure Sciences, Engineering, and Computer Science. This scholarship provides partial funding, covering tuition fees only. Eligible candidates must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, and experience with large-scale software projects is preferred. A strong PhD application relevant to the department's research is essential. The application deadline is October 25, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
