80ceeab1-a172-4561-974f-72a36bfb7135: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship at Imperial College London offers a stipend for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences and Computer Science. Open to EU, UK, and international students, the scholarship covers tuition and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, and demonstrate English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Preference is given to those with previous research awards. The application deadline is August 19, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
