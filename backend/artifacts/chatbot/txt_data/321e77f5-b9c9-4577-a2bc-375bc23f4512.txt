321e77f5-b9c9-4577-a2bc-375bc23f4512: The Dr Hooi Chew Scholarship is an undergraduate, merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Engineering in Australia. It provides funding of $8,500 per annum for up to 4 years to support students from rural or regional Australia facing financial hardship. Eligible applicants must be Australian Citizens or Permanent Residents. The application deadline is January 12, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/international.html.
