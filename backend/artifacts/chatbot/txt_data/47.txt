47: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom for international students, covering PhD studies in Computer Science and Engineering. The funding includes a stipend only, with a deadline for applications set for September 3, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, at least one year of research experience, a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90), and demonstrated academic excellence or innovation.
