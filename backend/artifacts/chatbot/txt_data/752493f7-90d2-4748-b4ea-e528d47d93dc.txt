752493f7-90d2-4748-b4ea-e528d47d93dc: The Bernard Lee Scholarship for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Students is an Indigenous scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia. It provides financial support of $8,500 per annum for up to 5 years for undergraduate, postgraduate coursework, and postgraduate research students. The scholarship aims to assist Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students and requires a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50. Applicants must have an unconditional offer of admission or be currently enrolled at the University of Sydney. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is September 30, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/bernard-lee.html.
