752493f7-90d2-4748-b4ea-e528d47d93dc: The Bernard Lee Scholarship for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Students is an Indigenous scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia. It provides financial support of $8,500 per annum for up to 5 years for undergraduate, postgraduate coursework, and postgraduate research students. The scholarship is open to all majors and requires applicants to have a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50, along with an unconditional offer of admission or current enrollment at the University of Sydney. Preference is given to applicants demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is September 30, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/bernard-lee.html.
