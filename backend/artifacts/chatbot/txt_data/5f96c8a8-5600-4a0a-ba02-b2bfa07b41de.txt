5f96c8a8-5600-4a0a-ba02-b2bfa07b41de: The Dr Hooi Chew Scholarship is a need-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Engineering. It provides funding of $8,500 per annum for up to four years to support students from rural or regional Australia facing financial hardship. Eligible majors include Science, Engineering, and Medicine and Health. The application deadline is January 12, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/international.html.
