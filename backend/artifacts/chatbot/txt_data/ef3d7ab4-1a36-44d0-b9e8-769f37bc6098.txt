ef3d7ab4-1a36-44d0-b9e8-769f37bc6098: The University of the Fraser Valley Regional Entrance Scholarship is a fee waiver/discount scholarship available to international students globally. It is offered by the University of the Fraser Valley in Canada and applies to all majors at the undergraduate and graduate levels. The scholarship provides CAD $5,000 towards tuition fees for the first year (2nd semester) of study. Eligible applicants must be new international students entering their first year and demonstrate academic excellence with a minimum CPGA of 3.0 in high school. The scholarship aims to support talented individuals and is open to students of any gender.
