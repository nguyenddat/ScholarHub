9a89b09c-c57b-4bfa-a9c4-67b952432364: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. It is available to EU and international students pursuing a PhD in Computer Science or Engineering. The scholarship provides a stipend to support living expenses. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and demonstrate academic excellence or innovation. Proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90) is required. The application deadline is September 3, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
