61: The University of Edinburgh offers a PhD scholarship in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing, available to international students in the United Kingdom. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science and Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, and preference is given to those interested in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards are considered advantageous. The application deadline is September 12, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
