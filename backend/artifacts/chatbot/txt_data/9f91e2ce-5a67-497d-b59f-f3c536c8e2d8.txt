9f91e2ce-5a67-497d-b59f-f3c536c8e2d8: The International Research Scholarship offered by the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) in New South Wales, Australia, covers tuition fees for up to 3.5 years. It is available to all international students enrolling full-time in a Higher Degree by Research (HDR) at UTS. Eligible applicants must be overseas students as defined by the Higher Education Support Act 2003 and must undertake study in a subject area identified by UTS as a research concentration. Applicants are required to purchase and maintain a standard Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) policy approved by the Commonwealth government Department of Health and Ageing. The scholarship is open to all genders and further details on experience and achievements can be found on the official UTS scholarship page. The application deadline is not specified and interested candidates should contact UTS for more information. More details can be accessed at the official link: https://www.uts.edu.au/scholarship/international-research-scholarship.
