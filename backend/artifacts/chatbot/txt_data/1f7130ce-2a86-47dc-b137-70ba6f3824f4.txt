1f7130ce-2a86-47dc-b137-70ba6f3824f4: The Lincoln University Doctoral Scholarships in New Zealand offer a fee waiver and a NZ $30,000 annual stipend for three years to international students. Sponsored by Lincoln University, this scholarship is available for all fields of study at the doctoral level. Applicants must demonstrate academic excellence with a minimum GPA of A- (80%) and have either newly registered or been registered for no more than six months in the doctoral program by the application deadline of 30 September 2025. Additional criteria include relevant research or experience. The scholarship is open to all genders globally. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.lincoln.ac.nz/study/scholarships/search-scholarships/lincoln-university-doctoral-scholarships/.
