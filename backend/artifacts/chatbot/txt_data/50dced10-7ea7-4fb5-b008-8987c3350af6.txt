50dced10-7ea7-4fb5-b008-8987c3350af6: The University of Otago Coursework Master's Scholarship is a fee waiver/discount scholarship for coursework-based Master's students at the University of Otago, New Zealand. It offers NZ$10,000 towards tuition fees and is open to all fields of study except for the Master of Business Administration. The selection criteria include academic excellence, relevant experience, and research paper evaluation. The application deadline is not specified; interested candidates should contact for consultation. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.otago.ac.nz/study/scholarships/university-of-otago-coursework-masters-scholarship).
