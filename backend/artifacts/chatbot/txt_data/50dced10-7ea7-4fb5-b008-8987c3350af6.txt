50dced10-7ea7-4fb5-b008-8987c3350af6: The University of Otago Coursework Master's Scholarship is a fee waiver/discount scholarship for coursework-based Master's students at the University of Otago in New Zealand, excluding those in the Master of Business Administration program. It offers NZ$10,000 towards tuition fees. The scholarship is open to all fields of study at the Master's degree level. Selection criteria include academic excellence, relevant experience, and research paper evaluation. The application deadline is not specified; applicants are advised to contact the university for details. For more information, visit the official link: https://www.otago.ac.nz/study/scholarships/university-of-otago-coursework-masters-scholarship.
