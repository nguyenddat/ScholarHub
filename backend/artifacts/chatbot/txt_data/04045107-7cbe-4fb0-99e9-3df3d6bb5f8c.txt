04045107-7cbe-4fb0-99e9-3df3d6bb5f8c: The JACE Foundation Scholarship is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, aimed at supporting students with Autism Spectrum Disorder. It provides funding of $8,500 per annum for up to three years. Eligible applicants must be Australian citizens with an unconditional offer of admission or currently enrolled at the University of Sydney, and must maintain a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50. The application deadline is January 12, 2025.
