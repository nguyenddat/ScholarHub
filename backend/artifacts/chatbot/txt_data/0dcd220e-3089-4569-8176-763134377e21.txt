0dcd220e-3089-4569-8176-763134377e21: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is offered by the University of Southampton in the UK for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, specifically in Computer Science. This scholarship provides a stipend and covers tuition fees. Eligible candidates must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, have an interest in interdisciplinary projects, and possess proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Preference is given to those with previous research awards or distinctions. The application deadline is September 9, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
