e2cf5140-4ab4-4612-97a8-753b771f2ada: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is offered by Imperial College London in the United Kingdom, targeting EU students. It supports PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. The scholarship provides a stipend and covers tuition fees. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, and demonstrate English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required. The application deadline is September 14, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
