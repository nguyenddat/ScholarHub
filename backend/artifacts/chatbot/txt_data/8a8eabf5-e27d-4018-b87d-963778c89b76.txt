8a8eabf5-e27d-4018-b87d-963778c89b76: The Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences Humanitarian Undergraduate Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney in Australia. It provides a fixed funding amount of $62,000 per annum for up to 4 years, covering tuition, living allowance, and OSHC. This scholarship is available for international students holding specific humanitarian visas, including Bridging Visa A, Bridging Visa E, Temporary Protection Visa, Safe Haven Enterprise Visa, and others. Eligible candidates must be commencing an undergraduate coursework degree in Arts and Social Sciences with a minimum GPA of 50. The application deadline is May 31, 2025.
