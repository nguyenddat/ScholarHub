9f63b375-1217-430d-878c-58d3c2e3ece5: The University of Leeds offers a Computational Biology Fully Funded PhD scholarship in the United Kingdom, open to EU and international students. This scholarship is for PhD studies in Engineering and Computer Science, providing partial funding that covers tuition fees. Applicants should have experience with large-scale software projects and an interest in interdisciplinary research. Academic excellence or innovation is also required, and GRE scores may be needed for some candidates. The application deadline is September 10, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
