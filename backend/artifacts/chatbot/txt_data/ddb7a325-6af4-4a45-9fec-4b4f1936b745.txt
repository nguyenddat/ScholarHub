ddb7a325-6af4-4a45-9fec-4b4f1936b745: The University of Canterbury Alumni Scholarship offers a fee waiver of NZ$5,000 for full-time undergraduate and postgraduate students at the University of Canterbury in New Zealand. It is open to all fields of study and available to candidates of any gender and nationality. Applicants must have completed or be completing a university entrance qualification and have been studying at the secondary level in the year of application. Selection criteria include academic excellence, involvement in sports, cultural or community activities, and alignment with the objectives of the University of Canterbury Alumni South Island Chapter. The application deadline is 15 August. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.hotcourses.vn/study/newzealand/school-college-university/university-of-canterbury/142318/international.html.
