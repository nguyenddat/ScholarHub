34a0cb72-6ecf-4755-a811-5e5a14a42e14: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship is offered by Imperial College London in the UK, open to all international students. This scholarship supports PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, specifically Computer Science, providing a stipend for living expenses. The application deadline is September 13, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, experience with large-scale software projects, interest in interdisciplinary research, and possibly GRE scores. Previous research awards or distinctions are also considered. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
