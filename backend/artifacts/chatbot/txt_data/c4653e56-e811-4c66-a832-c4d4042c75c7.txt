c4653e56-e811-4c66-a832-c4d4042c75c7: The University of Leeds offers PhD Scholarships in the School of Computer Science for EU and international students in the United Kingdom. This partial funding scholarship covers tuition fees and is available for those pursuing a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with an interest in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some, and previous research awards or distinctions are considered. The application deadline is August 3, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
