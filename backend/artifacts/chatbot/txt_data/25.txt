25: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Edinburgh in the United Kingdom for international students pursuing a PhD in Engineering. This scholarship provides partial funding, covering tuition fees only. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research, and proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is also required. The application deadline is November 2, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
