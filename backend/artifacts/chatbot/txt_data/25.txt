25: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a fully funded scholarship offered by the University of Cambridge in the UK for international students pursuing a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences, Engineering, or Computer Science. The funding covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, experience with large-scale software projects, and submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. Additionally, candidates should have published at least one peer-reviewed paper. The application deadline is November 1, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
