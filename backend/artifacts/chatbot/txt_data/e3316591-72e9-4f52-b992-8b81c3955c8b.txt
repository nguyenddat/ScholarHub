e3316591-72e9-4f52-b992-8b81c3955c8b: The **Học bổng Tôn vinh Thạc sỹ Lên lớp Quốc tế** is a merit-based scholarship offered by **Lincoln University** in **New Zealand**. It provides funding of up to **10,000 NZD** towards tuition fees for students enrolled in any **Master's** program that requires 180 credits. Applicants must have a minimum GPA of **75%** or a **B+** average in their final year of undergraduate studies. The selection criteria also consider experience, research, certifications, and achievements. The application deadline is not specified.
