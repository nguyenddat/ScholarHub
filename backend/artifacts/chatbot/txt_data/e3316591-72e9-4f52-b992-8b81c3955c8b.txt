It is available for students enrolling in any 180-credit taught master's program. The scholarship provides up to 10,000 NZD towards tuition fees. Eligible candidates must have a GPA of at least 75% or a B+ average in the final year of their bachelor's program. The selection criteria also consider experience, research, certification, and achievements. There is no specified application deadline.
