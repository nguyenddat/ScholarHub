40b1ff4a-fd39-4cbb-8c88-bf729de04a40: The Australia Awards Scholarships are fully funded scholarships provided by the Australian Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade for students from developing countries. They cover full tuition fees, return air travel, establishment allowance, and living expenses for Bachelor, Master's, and PhD degrees in various fields aligned with Australia's country priorities. The scholarships allow for full-time study at participating Australian institutions. The application deadline varies by country. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.goabroad.com/articles/scholarships-abroad/scholarships-for-study-abroad-around-the-world.
