06e19568-7b4f-470a-97cb-d184bdb10c64: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity provided by the University of Oxford for PhD studies in Engineering, specifically in Computer Science. It is open to international, UK, and EU students and covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must submit a research proposal aligned with department goals and demonstrate a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields. Proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90) and at least one peer-reviewed publication are required. The application deadline is October 8, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
