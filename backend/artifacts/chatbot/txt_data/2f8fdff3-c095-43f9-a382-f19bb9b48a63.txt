2f8fdff3-c095-43f9-a382-f19bb9b48a63: The Queen Elizabeth Commonwealth Scholarship 2025/26 is a fully funded scholarship offered by the Queen Elizabeth Commonwealth Scholarship Programme for students from Commonwealth countries to pursue a Master's degree at various Commonwealth nations. The scholarship covers full tuition fees, living expenses, return airfare, and an arrival allowance. The application deadline is June 4, 2025. Eligible majors include all fields of study, and while a Master's degree is required, specific experience or research criteria are not detailed.
