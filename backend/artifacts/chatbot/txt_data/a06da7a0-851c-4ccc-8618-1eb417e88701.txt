a06da7a0-851c-4ccc-8618-1eb417e88701: The PhD Studentship in Natural Language Processing is offered by Imperial College London in the United Kingdom. It is open to international, UK, and EU students pursuing a PhD in Engineering, specifically in Computer Science. The scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses and covers tuition fees. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and having at least one peer-reviewed publication is advantageous. The application deadline is August 29, 2025. More details can be found on the official website: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
