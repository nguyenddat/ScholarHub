b2b296c1-3747-4885-856a-9e4f3c9b46af: The Diana Inglis Carment Scholarship is a merit-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's School of Architecture, Design and Planning, Australia. It provides a fixed amount of $5,000 to honours level students pursuing the Bachelor of Design in Architecture (Honours). Applicants must have completed the requirements for the Bachelor of Design in Architecture or an equivalent degree and hold an unconditional offer for the honours program. Full-time enrollment in the semester following the award is required. Selection is based on academic merit, a research proposal, and a personal statement. The application deadline is 09 February 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/diana-inglis-carment-scholarship.html.
