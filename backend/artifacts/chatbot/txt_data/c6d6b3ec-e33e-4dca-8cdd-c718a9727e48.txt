It is available for vocational, undergraduate, and postgraduate students from CIS countries, Europe, Latin America, the Middle East, Africa, East Asia, Southeast Asia, and South Asia. The funding ranges from CAD$1,000.00 to CAD$2,000.00 and is awarded based on academic excellence and strong English language proficiency. Applicants must have an above-average GPA (B equivalent or higher) and strong English test scores (IELTS 7.0 or equivalent). The application deadlines are January 31 (Summer), May 31 (Fall), and September 30 (Winter). For more details, visit the official link: https://www.douglascollege.ca/international-students/prospective-students/scholarships-and-awards.
