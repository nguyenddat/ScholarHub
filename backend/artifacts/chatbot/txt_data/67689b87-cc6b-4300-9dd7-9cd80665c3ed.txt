67689b87-cc6b-4300-9dd7-9cd80665c3ed: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship at Imperial College London is a fully funded opportunity for international, EU, and UK students to pursue a PhD in Computer Science or Engineering in the United Kingdom. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required. The application deadline is July 21, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
