It provides full tuition coverage and health insurance. The scholarship is merit-based, focusing on academic excellence, and is available for all fields of study. Candidates are selected through a competitive ranking process. Eligibility requires confirmation of a qualified supervisor, available space and resources, and alignment with ANU's research strategy. The application deadlines are 15 April for the year of commencing the HDR program or 31 August for the year prior. For more details, visit the official link: https://study.anu.edu.au/scholarships/find-scholarship/research-training-program-rtp-international-fee-offset-scholarship."}
