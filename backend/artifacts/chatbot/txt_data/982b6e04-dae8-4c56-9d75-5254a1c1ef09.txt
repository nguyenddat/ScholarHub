982b6e04-dae8-4c56-9d75-5254a1c1ef09: The Adam Scott Foundation Scholarship is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for commencing students in Australia. It provides up to $20,000 per annum towards university accommodation for up to four years. The scholarship is open to all majors and is based on academic merit and financial need. Applicants must be under 30 years of age, 18 years old by 30 January 2025, and have received a MySydney Scholarship offer. They must also apply for a room in university accommodation. The selection criteria include having a course offer from the University of Sydney and demonstrating leadership skills through extracurricular activities. The application deadline is 30 November 2024. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/adam-scott-foundation-scholarship.html.
