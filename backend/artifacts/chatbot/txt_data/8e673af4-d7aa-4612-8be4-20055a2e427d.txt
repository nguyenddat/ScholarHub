8e673af4-d7aa-4612-8be4-20055a2e427d: The University of Glasgow offers partial funding for a PhD in Software Engineering, covering tuition fees. This scholarship is available to students from the UK, EU, and internationally, focusing on Computer Science. Applicants must submit a research proposal aligned with department goals and may need GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, along with experience in large-scale software projects. The application deadline is August 4, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
