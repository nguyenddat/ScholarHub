0296982e-cf65-4dfa-99ca-8cc3b48a8c52: The University of Glasgow offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to international students in the United Kingdom. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. It is open to candidates pursuing a PhD in Computer Science or Engineering. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience and submit a research proposal aligned with the department's goals. GRE scores may be required for some applicants, and academic excellence or innovation is highly valued. The application deadline is August 27, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
