38: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship is a fully funded scholarship offered by the University of Southampton in the United Kingdom. It supports PhD studies in Computer Science and related fields, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. Eligible candidates must have experience with large-scale software projects, a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research, and proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Additionally, applicants should have published at least one peer-reviewed paper. The application deadline is August 22, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
