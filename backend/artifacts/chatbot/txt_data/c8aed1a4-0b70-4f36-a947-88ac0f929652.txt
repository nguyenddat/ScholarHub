This scholarship supports PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences and Engineering, specifically in Computer Science. It provides a stipend to cover living expenses. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields and are required to make a compelling PhD application relevant to the department's research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred, and GRE scores may be required for some applicants. The application deadline is October 21, 2025.
