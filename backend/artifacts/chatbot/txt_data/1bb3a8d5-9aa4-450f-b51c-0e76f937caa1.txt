1bb3a8d5-9aa4-450f-b51c-0e76f937caa1: The Charles Herbert Currey Memorial Scholarship is a mobility scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences. It is available for undergraduate and postgraduate students enrolled in Arts and Social Sciences, supporting those who wish to participate in exchange or short-term mobility programs. The scholarship provides funding of up to $7,000 and is open to students from Australia and international partner universities. The application deadline is January 24, 2025. Selection criteria focus on enrollment in the relevant degree program, with no specific requirements for work experience, research, certifications, or achievements. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/charles-herbert-currey-memorial-scholarship.html.
