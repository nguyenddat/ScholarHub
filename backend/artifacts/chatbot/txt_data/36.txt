36: The University of Manchester offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to all international students, including those from the EU and the UK. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. Applicants must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch), submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Academic excellence or innovation is also considered. The application deadline is July 19, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
