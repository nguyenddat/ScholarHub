22: The University of Cambridge offers a fully funded PhD Studentship in Natural Language Processing, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. This opportunity is available for students in the UK, focusing on Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects and submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred, and GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is September 27, 2025.
