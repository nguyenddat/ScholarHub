9e0343ca-148b-4d3a-af60-9063d5043fd8: The International and Global Studies Travelling Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia. It provides a fixed amount of $4000 to students enrolled in the Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Advanced Studies (International and Global Studies) who intend to participate in an approved overseas exchange. Eligibility is limited to students at the University of Sydney in the specified degree program. The scholarship is awarded based on academic merit in specific units of study. For more details on the application deadline and additional information, please refer to the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/international-global-studies-travelling-scholarship.html.
