5: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom for PhD students in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. This scholarship provides partial funding, covering tuition fees only. Eligible candidates must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, and experience with large-scale software projects is preferred. Applicants are required to submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. The application deadline is August 20, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
