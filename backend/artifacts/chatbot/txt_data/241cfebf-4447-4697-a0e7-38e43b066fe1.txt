241cfebf-4447-4697-a0e7-38e43b066fe1: The International Research Scholarship is a tuition fee coverage scholarship for international students enrolling full-time in a Higher Degree by Research (HDR) at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) in New South Wales, Australia. It is available for all fields of study and covers tuition fees for up to 3.5 years. Applicants must be overseas students as defined by the Higher Education Support Act 2003 and must maintain a standard OSHC policy. Additional selection criteria include relevant experience and research in designated areas of concentration at UTS. For more details, refer to the official link: [View details](https://www.uts.edu.au/scholarship/international-research-scholarship). The application deadline is to be confirmed by contacting the provider.
