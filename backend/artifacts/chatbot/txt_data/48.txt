48: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a partial funding opportunity for PhD students at the University of Oxford in the UK, covering tuition fees. It is aimed at candidates pursuing studies in Applied and Pure Sciences or Engineering. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. A Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related field is required, along with demonstrated academic excellence or innovation. The application deadline is October 28, 2025.
