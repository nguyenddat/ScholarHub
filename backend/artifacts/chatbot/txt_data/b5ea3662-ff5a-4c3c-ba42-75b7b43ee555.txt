b5ea3662-ff5a-4c3c-ba42-75b7b43ee555: The Jerome De Costa Memorial Award is a graduate exhibition scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences in Australia. It provides a fixed amount of $5,000 to undergraduate students enrolled in the Bachelor of Visual Arts or Bachelor of Visual Arts and Bachelor of Advanced Studies programs, who are completing their last studio unit in the year of application. Applicants must exhibit their work as part of the graduate exhibition and demonstrate financial hardship. The application deadline is May 26, 2025. Selection criteria include academic merit, portfolio quality, financial need, and a personal statement detailing how the scholarship will support their exhibition work. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/the-jerome-de-costa-memorial-undergraduate-degree-show-bursary.html.
