0d241d17-08cf-45ea-920d-0dad824e03c6: The International Research Scholarship offered by the University of Technology Sydney in New South Wales, Australia, covers tuition fees for up to 3.5 years for international students enrolling full-time in a Higher Degree by Research (HDR). It is open to all fields of study at the postgraduate level. Eligible applicants must be overseas students as defined by the Higher Education Support Act 2003 and must undertake study in a research area identified by the university. Applicants are required to purchase and maintain a standard OSHC policy approved by the Commonwealth government Department of Health and Ageing. The scholarship is available to all genders. For more details on experience and achievements criteria, refer to the official link. The application deadline is not specified; contact the university for more information. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.uts.edu.au/scholarship/international-research-scholarship.
