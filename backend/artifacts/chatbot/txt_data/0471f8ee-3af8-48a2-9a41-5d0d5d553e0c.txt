0471f8ee-3af8-48a2-9a41-5d0d5d553e0c: The University of Oxford offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science for students from the EU and all international regions. These scholarships cover tuition fees and provide a living stipend, supporting studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. Applicants must pursue full-time study and have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields. A research proposal aligned with department goals is required, and GRE scores may be necessary for some candidates. Applicants should hold a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. The application deadline is June 18, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
