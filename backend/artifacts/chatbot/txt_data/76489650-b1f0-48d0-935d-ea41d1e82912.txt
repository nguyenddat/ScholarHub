76489650-b1f0-48d0-935d-ea41d1e82912: The University of Edinburgh offers a Computational Biology Fully Funded PhD scholarship for international and UK students. This scholarship is for a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences, specifically in Computer Science, and provides partial funding covering tuition fees. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, along with proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). The application deadline is July 8, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
