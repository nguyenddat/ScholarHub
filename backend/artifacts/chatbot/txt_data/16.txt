16: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a fully funded scholarship offered by the University of Cambridge in the UK for international students. It covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, Engineering, and Computer Science. The application deadline is November 1, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, experience with large-scale software projects, a research proposal aligned with department goals, possession of Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Computer Science or related fields, and at least one published peer-reviewed paper. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
