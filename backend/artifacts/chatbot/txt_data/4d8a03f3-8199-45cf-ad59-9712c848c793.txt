4d8a03f3-8199-45cf-ad59-9712c848c793: The University of Edinburgh offers a Computational Biology Fully Funded PhD scholarship for international and UK students. This scholarship is for a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences, specifically in Computer Science, and provides partial funding covering tuition fees. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, along with proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). The application deadline is July 8, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
