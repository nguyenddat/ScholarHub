44: The University of Cambridge offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to all international students, including those from the EU and UK. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible candidates should have a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related field, with a preference for those with experience in large-scale software projects and an interest in interdisciplinary research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is also required. The application deadline is July 5, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
