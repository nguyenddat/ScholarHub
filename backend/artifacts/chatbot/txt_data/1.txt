1: The University of Oxford offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science for international students, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. Eligible fields include Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. Applicants must have a strong academic background in relevant fields and submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and a first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred. The application deadline is June 18, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
