2dd78f60-88c9-475f-bc4d-829f269f7a98: The Research Training Program (RTP) International Fee Offset Scholarship is offered by The Australian National University in the Australian Capital Territory, Australia. It is a full tuition and health insurance scholarship for international students pursuing postgraduate degrees. The scholarship is merit-based, focusing on academic excellence, and is awarded through a competitive ranking process. Eligible candidates must have their research aligned with ANU's research strategy and have a qualified supervisor, space, and resources available. The application deadlines are 15 April for those commencing their HDR Program in the same year, or 31 August for those starting in the following year. For more details, visit the official link: https://study.anu.edu.au/scholarships/find-scholarship/research-training-program-rtp-international-fee-offset-scholarship.
