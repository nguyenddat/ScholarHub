5e03dfab-678e-4f16-a682-8eb2dfb52faa: The University of Oxford offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. These scholarships are available to EU and international students interested in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required. The application deadline is June 18, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
