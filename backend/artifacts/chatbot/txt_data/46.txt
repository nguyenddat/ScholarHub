46: The PhD Studentship in Natural Language Processing is offered by Imperial College London in the United Kingdom. This scholarship is available for international students and covers a stipend only, with no tuition coverage mentioned. It is aimed at candidates pursuing a PhD in Engineering. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. Additional criteria include the potential requirement of GRE scores and having published at least one peer-reviewed paper. The application deadline is August 29, 2025.
