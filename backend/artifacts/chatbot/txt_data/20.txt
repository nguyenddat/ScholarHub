20: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a stipend-only scholarship offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. It is available for international students pursuing a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences or Engineering. The funding covers tuition and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. GRE scores may be required for some applicants. The application deadline is October 21, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
