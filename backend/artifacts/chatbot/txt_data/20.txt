20: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is a partial funding opportunity provided by the University of Cambridge in the UK for international students pursuing a PhD in Computer Science. The scholarship covers tuition fees and has a deadline for applications on September 27, 2025. Eligible candidates should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some applicants, and demonstrated academic excellence or innovation is also considered. For more details, visit the official link.
