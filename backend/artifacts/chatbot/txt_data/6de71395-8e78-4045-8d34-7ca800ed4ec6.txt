6de71395-8e78-4045-8d34-7ca800ed4ec6: The Adam Scott Foundation Scholarship is a need-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for undergraduate students in Australia. It provides funding of up to $20,000 per annum towards university accommodation for a maximum of four years. Eligible applicants must be under 30 years old at the time of application, have received a MySydney Scholarship offer, and be at least 18 years old by January 30, 2025. The selection criteria include academic merit, demonstrated leadership in extracurricular activities, and involvement in community service. The application deadline is November 30, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/adam-scott-foundation-scholarship.html.
