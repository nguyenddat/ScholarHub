0761a529-325c-4da2-8a8f-2455017dbc19: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship, provided by the University of Oxford in the UK, offers partial funding for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences and Engineering. This scholarship covers tuition fees and is available to students with a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields. Applicants should have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in relevant areas and demonstrate academic excellence or innovation. The scholarship encourages interest in interdisciplinary projects. The application deadline is October 28, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
