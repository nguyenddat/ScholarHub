b46be3a5-d452-4695-aba1-dff5c9f6ceae: The JACE Foundation Scholarship is a disability support scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for undergraduate students with Autism Spectrum Disorder. It provides funding of $8,500 per annum for up to three years. Applicants must be formally diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder and registered with the Inclusion and Disability Services at the university. The scholarship is open to all majors and requires a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50 each semester. The application deadline is January 12, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/jace-foundation-scholarship.html.
