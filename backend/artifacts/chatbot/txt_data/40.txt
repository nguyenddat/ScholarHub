40: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are fully funded PhD opportunities offered by the University of Manchester in the United Kingdom. These scholarships cover tuition fees and provide a living stipend for international students pursuing studies in Engineering, Computer Science, and Applied and Pure Sciences. The application deadline is August 27, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, knowledge of machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch), interest in interdisciplinary projects, and possession of Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in relevant fields. Demonstrated academic excellence or innovation is also preferred. For more details, visit the official link.
