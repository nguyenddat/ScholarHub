252dfc5b-6e6f-4463-96e3-dbdc32413ea6: The Lincoln University Doctoral Scholarships in New Zealand offer a fee waiver and a NZ $30,000 annual stipend for three years to international students pursuing postgraduate degrees in any field. Applicants must have a minimum GPA of 80% (A- average) and relevant research experience. The scholarship is available to those new to the Lincoln University Doctoral program or registered for no more than six months prior to the application deadline of September 30, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.lincoln.ac.nz/study/scholarships/search-scholarships/lincoln-university-doctoral-scholarships/
