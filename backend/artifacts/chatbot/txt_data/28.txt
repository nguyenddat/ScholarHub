28: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a partial scholarship offered by the University of Manchester in the United Kingdom for PhD studies in Engineering. It covers tuition fees and has a deadline for applications on August 16, 2025. Eligible candidates should have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, and must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch. Applicants are required to submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals and show evidence of academic excellence or innovation. For more details, visit the official link.
