28: The University of Southampton offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology for international students, including those from the EU. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants should demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and having at least one peer-reviewed publication is preferred. The application deadline is August 2, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
