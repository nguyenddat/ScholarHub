975f9cd8-445c-4918-ad0c-ad7ab8501fbf: The International Entrance Award is offered by the University of Prince Edward Island in Canada for global applicants. It is available for undergraduate and postgraduate students across all fields of study. This non-renewable award covers tuition fees and is reviewed upon admission by the Scholarships and Awards Committee. The selection criteria include educational background, personal attributes, and achievements, with specific details on experience, research, and certification available on the official website. The scholarship is open to all international students regardless of gender. Applicants should refer to the original URL for the application deadline and further details: https://www.upei.ca/scholarships-and-awards/display?awardid=463.
