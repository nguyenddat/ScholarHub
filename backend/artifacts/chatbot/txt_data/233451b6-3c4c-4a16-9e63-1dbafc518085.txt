233451b6-3c4c-4a16-9e63-1dbafc518085: The DAAD EPOS Scholarship 2026/27 is a fully funded scholarship provided by the German Academic Exchange Service for international students pursuing Master's, MPhil, MBA, LLM, or PhD programs in development-related fields in Germany. The scholarship offers a monthly stipend ranging from €861 to €1,200, along with a travel allowance, health insurance, and study materials for a duration of 12-42 months. The application deadline varies by program. Selection criteria focus primarily on educational qualifications, with no specific requirements for experience, research, certification, or achievements.
