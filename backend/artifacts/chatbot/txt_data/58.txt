58: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship, offered by the University of Glasgow in the UK, provides partial funding for PhD studies in Engineering and Applied and Pure Sciences. The scholarship covers tuition fees and is open to all international students. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields and submit a compelling PhD application relevant to the department's research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is June 27, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
