dcbd0cb3-bebf-438e-9c55-76fe23625459: The Research Training Program (RTP) International Fee Offset Scholarship is offered by The Australian National University in Australia. It is a full tuition and health insurance scholarship for international students pursuing postgraduate degrees. The scholarship is merit-based, focusing on academic excellence, and is available for all fields of study. Candidates are selected through a competitive ranking process. The application deadlines are 15 April for those commencing their HDR Program in the same year, or 31 August for those starting in the following year. Selection criteria include academic excellence, research alignment with ANU's strategy, and confirmation of a qualified supervisor. For more details, visit the official link: https://study.anu.edu.au/scholarships/find-scholarship/research-training-program-rtp-international-fee-offset-scholarship.
