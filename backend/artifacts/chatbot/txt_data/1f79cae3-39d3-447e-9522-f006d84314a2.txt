1f79cae3-39d3-447e-9522-f006d84314a2: The Excellence Scholarships at Loughborough University, UK, offer a 20% tuition fee waiver for all international postgraduate students enrolled in Taught Programmes starting in October or January, excluding certain departments. Applicants must be self-funding students with a first-class degree or equivalent and not receiving other university awards, except the Alumni Bursary. The selection criteria include academic excellence, experience, research, certification, and achievements. The application deadline is to be confirmed by contacting the university. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.lboro.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/fees-funding/masters-funding/excellence-scholarship/
