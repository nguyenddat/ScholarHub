ea9290f6-9d77-4852-9c31-6fe825cacd83: The Thomas Henry Coulson Scholarship is a need-based undergraduate scholarship for students studying English at the University of Sydney, Australia. It offers a fixed amount of $5,500 to support those facing disadvantage or hardship. Established in 2020, it is funded by a donation from 1916. Eligible applicants must be in their 2nd, 3rd, or 4th year of study and maintain a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50 each semester. The application deadline is 14 April 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/b/the-thomas-henry-coulson-scholarship.html.
