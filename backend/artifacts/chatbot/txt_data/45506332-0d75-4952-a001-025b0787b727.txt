45506332-0d75-4952-a001-025b0787b727: The Dr Lena Cansdale Scholarship is a merit-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney in Australia, specifically for students enrolled in Hebrew, Biblical, and Jewish Studies. The scholarship provides a fixed amount of $3,000 and requires applicants to have completed either two units of study in one semester or four units in one year, with a distinction average in relevant courses. The application deadline is March 7, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/the-dr-lena-cansdale-scholarship.html.
