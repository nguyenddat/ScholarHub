51ca26ee-180d-42fe-8f8e-7fd03f5be2e7: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Birmingham in the United Kingdom for PhD studies in Engineering, specifically in Computer Science. This scholarship is open to both UK and international students and provides a stipend for living expenses. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience and an interest in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and having received a previous research award or distinction is advantageous. The application deadline is August 19, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
