9f2acf21-49f9-4947-aee3-899ed2eb5d7c: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is offered by the University of Cambridge in the UK for international students. It provides partial funding covering tuition for a PhD in Computer Science. The scholarship is open to all intakes for full-time study and requires a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields. Applicants with an interest in interdisciplinary projects are preferred, and GRE scores may be required for some. Demonstrated academic excellence or innovation is also considered. The application deadline is September 27, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
