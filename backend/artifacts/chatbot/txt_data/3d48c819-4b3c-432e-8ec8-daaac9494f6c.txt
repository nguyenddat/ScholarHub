3d48c819-4b3c-432e-8ec8-daaac9494f6c: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are fully funded PhD scholarships offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. These scholarships cover tuition fees and provide a living stipend for international students pursuing a PhD in Computer Science. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, with a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. Experience with large-scale software projects and an interest in interdisciplinary research are preferred. The application deadline is October 17, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
