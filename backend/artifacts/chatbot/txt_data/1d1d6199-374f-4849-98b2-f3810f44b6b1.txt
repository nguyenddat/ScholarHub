1d1d6199-374f-4849-98b2-f3810f44b6b1: The University of Manchester offers a Computational Biology Fully Funded PhD scholarship in the UK, covering tuition and providing a living stipend. This opportunity is available for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, Engineering, and Computer Science. Applicants must have Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Computer Science or related fields, experience with large-scale software projects, and a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research. Previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is October 25, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
