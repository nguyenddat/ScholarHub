753e531e-abba-441a-b064-e3eb567cf1bf: The Charles Herbert Currey Memorial Scholarship is a funding opportunity provided by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences for undergraduate or postgraduate students. Located in Australia, this scholarship offers a fixed amount of up to $7,000. Eligible candidates must be enrolled in an undergraduate degree within FASS and intend to participate in an exchange or short-term mobility program, or be students from interstate or international partner universities undertaking units at the University of Sydney. Applicants must demonstrate financial need and provide a personal statement detailing their circumstances. The application deadline is January 24, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/charles-herbert-currey-memorial-scholarship.html.
