753e531e-abba-441a-b064-e3eb567cf1bf: The Charles Herbert Currey Memorial Scholarship is offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences for undergraduate or postgraduate students. This scholarship provides a fixed amount of up to $7,000 and is available to students enrolled in an undergraduate degree within FASS who intend to participate in an exchange program or short-term mobility program. Eligible candidates must demonstrate financial need and may include students from interstate or international partner universities. The application deadline is January 24, 2025. Selection criteria include academic merit, evidence of enrollment at a partner university, and a personal statement detailing financial hardship. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/charles-herbert-currey-memorial-scholarship.html.
