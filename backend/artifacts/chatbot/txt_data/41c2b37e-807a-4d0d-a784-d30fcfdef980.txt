41c2b37e-807a-4d0d-a784-d30fcfdef980: The Swinburne International Pathway Scholarship is a merit-based fee waiver offered by Swinburne University of Technology in Australia. It is available to international students starting a pathway intake in Foundation Year, UniLink diploma, or Postgraduate Qualifying Program from June 2024 to December 2024 at the Hawthorn campus. Eligible students can receive up to AUD $3,000 off tuition fees. The scholarship covers all fields of study at vocational, undergraduate, and postgraduate levels. Selection criteria include academic excellence, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with specific details available on the official website. The application deadline is to contact the university for consultation.
