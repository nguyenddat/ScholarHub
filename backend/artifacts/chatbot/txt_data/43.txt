43: The University of Glasgow offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology for international students. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science and Engineering. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. Demonstrated academic excellence or innovation is also considered. The application deadline is August 27, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
