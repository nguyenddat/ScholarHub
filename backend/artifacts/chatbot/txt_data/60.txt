60: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship is offered by Imperial College London in the United Kingdom. This scholarship is available for both EU and international students pursuing a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences or Computer Science. It provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is August 19, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
