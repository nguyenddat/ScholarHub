b028907f-de55-4f5a-9b61-06f18c706b50: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship, provided by University College London, offers partial funding for PhD studies in the UK, specifically covering tuition fees. It is open to international, EU, and UK students pursuing degrees in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. The scholarship emphasizes research in Computer Science and requires applicants to submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. Selection criteria include full-time study, experience with large-scale software projects, and potentially GRE scores. Applicants should hold a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. The application deadline is August 4, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
