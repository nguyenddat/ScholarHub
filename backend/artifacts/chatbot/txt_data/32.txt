32: The PhD Studentship in Natural Language Processing at the University of Cambridge offers a fully funded opportunity for students in the UK, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. This scholarship is available for candidates pursuing a PhD in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects and submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred, and GRE scores may be required for some applicants. The application deadline is September 27, 2025.
