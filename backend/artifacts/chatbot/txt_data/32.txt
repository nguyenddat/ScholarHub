32: The University of Birmingham offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science for students from the EU, UK, and all international regions. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects, submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is June 23, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
