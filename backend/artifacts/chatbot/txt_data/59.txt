59: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship is a funding opportunity provided by Imperial College London for international students pursuing a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences or Computer Science. The scholarship offers a stipend to cover living expenses, with tuition fees included. Applicants must have experience with large-scale software projects and a strong interest in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards are considered advantageous. The application deadline is September 13, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
