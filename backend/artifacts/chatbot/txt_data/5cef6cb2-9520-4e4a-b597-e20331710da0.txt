5cef6cb2-9520-4e4a-b597-e20331710da0: The Lendlease Bradfield Urbanisation Scholarship is a merit-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences in Australia. Valued at $10,000, it supports students in urbanism-related fields such as urban development, planning, design, and project management. Eligible candidates must be in their first or second year of study, have a minimum WAM of 65, and not have completed more than 96 credit points. The scholarship aims to foster future visionaries for Sydney's urban landscape. The application deadline is September 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/e/lendlease-bradfield-urbanisation-scholarship.html.
