1c1a6270-c4cf-4a57-984a-d63042a5a3ad: The Chancellor's Award is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, providing $10,000 to outstanding school leavers with an ATAR of 99.90 or equivalent. It is available for all undergraduate majors and is open to domestic students who have the University of Sydney as a preference on their UAC application. Automatic consideration is given to eligible applicants, who must be current school leavers. The scholarship emphasizes high academic achievement in the NSW Higher School Certificate Examination. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/chancellors-award.html.
