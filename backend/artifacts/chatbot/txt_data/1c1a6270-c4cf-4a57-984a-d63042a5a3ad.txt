1c1a6270-c4cf-4a57-984a-d63042a5a3ad: The Chancellor's Award is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, providing $10,000 for undergraduate students. It is available to domestic school leavers who achieve an ATAR of 99.90 or equivalent in the NSW Higher School Certificate Examination. Applicants must include a University of Sydney course in their UAC application preferences. There is no specified application deadline as consideration is automatic. Selection criteria focus on academic performance, with no additional experience or research requirements. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/chancellors-award.html.
