27: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity provided by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. It supports PhD studies in Computer Science and related fields, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. Eligible candidates should have a strong academic background in Computer Science, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. Applicants may need to submit GRE scores and should have at least one peer-reviewed publication. The application deadline is September 15, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
