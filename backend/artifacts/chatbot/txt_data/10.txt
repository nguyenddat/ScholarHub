10: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a partial funding opportunity for PhD studies in Engineering and Applied/Pure Sciences at University College London, UK. It covers tuition fees and is open to all international students. Applicants must have a first-class or upper second-class honours degree and experience with large-scale software projects. A research proposal aligned with department goals is required, and GRE scores may be necessary for some candidates. The application deadline is August 4, 2025.
