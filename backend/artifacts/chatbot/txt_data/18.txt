18: The University of Southampton offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to all international students in the UK. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research, and may need to provide GRE scores. A minimum of one published peer-reviewed paper is also preferred. The application deadline is August 2, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
