c6e1092c-2004-4dc3-8aa9-06348fba8908: The University of Oxford offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. These scholarships are available to EU and international students interested in pursuing research in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, and are required to submit a research proposal aligned with the department's goals. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred, and GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is June 18, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
