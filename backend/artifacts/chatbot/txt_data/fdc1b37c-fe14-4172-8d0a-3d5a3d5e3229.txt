fdc1b37c-fe14-4172-8d0a-3d5a3d5e3229: The George Harris Scholarship in Chemistry Honours is a need-based scholarship offered by the School of Chemistry at the University of Sydney, Australia. It is available for undergraduate students pursuing an Honours degree in Chemistry, providing funding of $8,500 per annum for up to one year. Established in 2023, the scholarship aims to promote new discoveries in Chemistry, with a preference for Aboriginal or Torres Strait Islander students. The application deadline is February 9, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/international/bachelors-honours/general.html.
