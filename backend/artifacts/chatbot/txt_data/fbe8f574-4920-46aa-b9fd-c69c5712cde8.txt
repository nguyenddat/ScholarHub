This partial funding scholarship covers tuition fees and is open to candidates pursuing a PhD in Computer Science, Engineering, or Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and submit a research proposal aligned with department goals. Previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is November 12, 2025.
