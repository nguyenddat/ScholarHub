41: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Glasgow in the UK for PhD students in Engineering, Applied, and Pure Sciences. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects, an interest in interdisciplinary research, and possess a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. Proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90) is also required. The application deadline is November 8, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
