521fb800-785c-4834-b21f-7502c81cb0de: The Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences Internship Scholarship is a fixed amount scholarship of $5000 offered by the University of Sydney in Australia. It is available for undergraduate and postgraduate students enrolled in the Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences who are undertaking an approved internship of at least 95 hours. Applicants must demonstrate financial hardship. The application deadline is April 16, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/arts-and-social-sciences-internship-scholarship.html.
