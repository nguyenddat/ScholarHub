1091f457-821d-4c80-80a5-4eb3a15cc65b: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a partial scholarship offered by University College London for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, specifically in Computer Science. It is available to international, UK, and EU students and covers tuition fees. The scholarship is aimed at candidates with a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, and those interested in interdisciplinary research projects. GRE scores may be required for some applicants, and preference is given to those with previous research awards or distinctions. The application deadline is September 10, 2025. More details can be found on the official website: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
