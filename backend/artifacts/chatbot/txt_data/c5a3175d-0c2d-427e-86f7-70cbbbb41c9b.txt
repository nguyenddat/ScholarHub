c5a3175d-0c2d-427e-86f7-70cbbbb41c9b: The Computational Biology Fully Funded PhD scholarship is offered by the University of Manchester in the United Kingdom. It is available to EU, international, and UK students pursuing a PhD in Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, or Engineering. This scholarship covers full tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must apply by July 19, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research, proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90), and demonstrated academic excellence or innovation. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
