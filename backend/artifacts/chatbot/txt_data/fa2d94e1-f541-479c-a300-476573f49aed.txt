fa2d94e1-f541-479c-a300-476573f49aed: The Dialysis Australia Nursing Scholarship is a need-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Susan Wakil School of Nursing and Midwifery in Australia. It supports undergraduate nursing students demonstrating financial need or social disadvantage, providing $32,500 per annum for up to 3 years. Applicants must maintain a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50 and be enrolled full-time. The application deadline is October 23, 2024, for Research Periods 1 and 2, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/c/dialysis_australia_nursing_scholarship.html.
