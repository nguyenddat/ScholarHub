b75c78cd-0e33-4440-a5e0-86752bf45af4: The Ernest Heine Family Foundation Digital Music Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for undergraduate students pursuing a Bachelor of Music in the Digital Music and Media Program. Located in Australia, this scholarship provides funding of $20,000 per annum for up to four years. Automatic consideration is given to applicants with an unconditional offer for the specified degree. The selection criteria include a minimum GPA of 70, but no specific work experience, research, or certifications are required. The scholarship was established in 2023. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/a/ernest-heine-family-foundation-digital-music-scholarship.html.
