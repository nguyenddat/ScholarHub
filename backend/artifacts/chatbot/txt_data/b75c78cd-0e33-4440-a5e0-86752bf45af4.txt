b75c78cd-0e33-4440-a5e0-86752bf45af4: The Ernest Heine Family Foundation Digital Music Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for undergraduate students pursuing a Bachelor of Music in the Digital Music and Media Program. Located in Australia, this scholarship provides funding of $20,000 per annum for up to four years. Applicants must hold an unconditional offer for the specified degree at the Sydney Conservatorium of Music. The selection criteria include a minimum GPA of 70, with no specific requirements for work experience, research, certifications, or achievements. The scholarship has an automatic consideration process with no specified application deadline. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/a/ernest-heine-family-foundation-digital-music-scholarship.html.
