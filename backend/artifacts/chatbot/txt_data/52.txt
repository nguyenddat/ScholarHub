52: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are fully funded PhD opportunities in Computer Science at the University of Cambridge, UK. They cover tuition fees and provide a living stipend. Open to all international applicants, the deadline for application is October 17, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, experience with large-scale software projects, interest in interdisciplinary research, and possession of Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for first-class or upper second-class honours degrees.
