3bcff85d-1f43-4df9-b5d3-5886d35a39fc: The International Scholarship offered by Loughborough University in the United Kingdom is a partial scholarship for undergraduate students from various majors. It provides up to a 25% reduction in tuition fees for the first year. This scholarship is available to international students globally, who are automatically considered upon application. The scholarship also offers additional support funds for music and sports talents, with musicians eligible for up to 500 GBP for music tuition and sports talents receiving up to 5,000 GBP annually for training if they achieve a basic international level in their sport. The selection criteria include education, personal attributes, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with specific details available on the official website.
