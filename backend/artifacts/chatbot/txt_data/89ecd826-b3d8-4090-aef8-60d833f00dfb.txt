89ecd826-b3d8-4090-aef8-60d833f00dfb: The University of Edinburgh offers PhD scholarships in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing for international students. This opportunity is available in the United Kingdom and focuses on Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences. The scholarship covers tuition and provides a living stipend. Applicants must pursue full-time PhD studies and have at least one year of research experience. Preference is given to those interested in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some applicants, and having a previous research award or distinction is advantageous. The application deadline is September 12, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
