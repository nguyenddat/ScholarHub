cf70967e-02ec-402e-8370-c66e9abc2411: The Digital Health Internship is a scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty scholarships, located in Australia. It is available for both undergraduate and postgraduate students enrolled in a coursework degree at the university. The scholarship provides funding of $9,000 for a 12-week internship in the field of digital health, supported by the Digital Health Cooperative Research Centre (DHCRC). Applicants must be currently enrolled at the University of Sydney and are required to complete an internship in digital health. The application deadline is October 22, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/digital-health-internship.html.
