14: The University of Edinburgh offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to international students in the UK. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, along with proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). The application deadline is July 8, 2025.
