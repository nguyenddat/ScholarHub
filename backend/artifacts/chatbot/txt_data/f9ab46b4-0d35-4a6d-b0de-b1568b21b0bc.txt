f9ab46b4-0d35-4a6d-b0de-b1568b21b0bc: The JW and BK Elkins Architectural Award is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Sydney School of Architecture Design and Planning. It is available for undergraduate (Honours) students in Architecture, providing $1,500 to assist with research or travel during their Honours year. Applicants must have completed the Bachelor of Design in Architecture at the University of Sydney and hold an unconditional offer for the Honours program. A research proposal is required, and academic merit will be considered. The application deadline is February 9, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/jw-bk-elkins-architectural-scholarship.html.
