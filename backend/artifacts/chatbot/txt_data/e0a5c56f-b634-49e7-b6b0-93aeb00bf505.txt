This scholarship provides a fixed amount of $1,000 for undergraduate and postgraduate coursework students in their final year, specifically those pursuing a Bachelor of Education (Primary Education) Honours or a Master of Teaching (Primary) with an interest in primary curriculum development. The application deadline is May 5, 2025. Selection criteria include academic merit and demonstrated interest in primary curriculum development.
