This scholarship provides a fixed amount of $1,000 for undergraduate and postgraduate coursework students in their final year, specifically those studying primary education with an interest in primary curriculum development. Eligible candidates must demonstrate academic merit and a commitment to the field. The application deadline is 5 May 2025.
