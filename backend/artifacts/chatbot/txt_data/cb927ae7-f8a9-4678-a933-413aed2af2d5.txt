cb927ae7-f8a9-4678-a933-413aed2af2d5: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship is offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. This scholarship is available to international, EU, and UK students pursuing a PhD in Computer Science or Engineering. It provides a stipend to support living expenses. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some applicants, and having at least one peer-reviewed publication is advantageous. The application deadline is October 20, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
