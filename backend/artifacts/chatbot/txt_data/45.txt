45: The University of Leeds offers partial funding PhD scholarships in Computer Science and Engineering for international students. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. Additionally, having at least one published peer-reviewed paper is preferred. The application deadline is July 30, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
