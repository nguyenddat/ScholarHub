8ffd5ad8-2b1d-4a71-9123-82dee7f6cbb9: The Garton Scholarship No II for French is a merit-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences. It is available to third-year students majoring in French, providing a fixed amount of $5,000 for one year. The scholarship is awarded to the student with the highest average mark in FRNC3633 and an additional 6 credit points. Applicants must have a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 65. There are no specified requirements for work experience, voluntary work, or research. The application deadline is not provided. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/b/garton-scholarship-2-french.html).
