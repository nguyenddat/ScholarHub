42: The University of Edinburgh offers PhD Scholarships in the School of Computer Science, available to students from the EU and the United Kingdom. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science and Engineering. Applicants must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is November 4, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
