1d17db8a-10b7-4ce0-8dd5-27c03624d1c5: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom for international and EU students. This scholarship supports PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences and Engineering, specifically in Computer Science. It provides a stipend to cover living expenses. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, and a first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred. GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The scholarship is open to all intakes for full-time study, and applicants must make a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. The application deadline is October 21, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
