7be03811-0b46-4254-b261-634d9151e6ac: The Diana Inglis Carment Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's School of Architecture, Design and Planning in Australia. It provides $5,000 in funding for undergraduate (Honours) students majoring in Architecture. To be eligible, applicants must have completed the Bachelor of Design in Architecture or an equivalent degree and hold an unconditional offer for the Honours program. Full-time enrollment in the semester following the award is required. Selection is based on academic merit, a research proposal, and a personal statement. The application deadline is February 9, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/diana-inglis-carment-scholarship.html.
