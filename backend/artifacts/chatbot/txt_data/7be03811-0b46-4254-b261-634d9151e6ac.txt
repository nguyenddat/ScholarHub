7be03811-0b46-4254-b261-634d9151e6ac: The Diana Inglis Carment Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's School of Architecture, Design and Planning, Australia. It provides $5,000 to undergraduate (Honours) students majoring in Architecture. Applicants must have completed the Bachelor of Design in Architecture or an equivalent degree and hold an unconditional offer for the Honours program. The scholarship is awarded based on academic merit, a research proposal, and a personal statement. Full-time enrollment in the semester following the award is required. The application deadline is February 9, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/diana-inglis-carment-scholarship.html.
