e655de78-9ae7-4d7d-a46f-36845a035da3: The SCA Alumni Fauvette Loureiro Memorial Scholarship is a graduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences in Australia. It provides funding of either $10,000 or $30,000 to eligible graduates from the Sydney College of the Arts (SCA) who are emerging or mid-career practitioners. Applicants must not be currently enrolled, have deferred their degree, or be research students. The scholarship considers artistic and academic merit, previous work, project descriptions, and budgets. The application deadline is May 19, 2025, at 5pm. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/fauvette-loureiro-memorial-scholarship-travel.html.
