e655de78-9ae7-4d7d-a46f-36845a035da3: The SCA Alumni Fauvette Loureiro Memorial Scholarship is a graduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty of Arts and Social Sciences in Australia. It provides a fixed funding amount of either $10,000 or $30,000. Eligible applicants must be graduates of the Sydney College of the Arts (SCA) and meet specific criteria related to their career stage, either as emerging or mid-career practitioners. Applicants should not be currently enrolled, have deferred their degree, or have previously received the scholarship in the last five years. The selection will consider artistic and academic merit, previous work, project descriptions, and budgets. The application deadline is May 19, 2025, at 5 PM. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/fauvette-loureiro-memorial-scholarship-travel.html).
