d833c882-6be3-40e8-acbf-9486f708dc4f: The Monash International Merit Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by Monash University in Australia. It is available for both Bachelor's and Master's degree students from any country, providing funding of up to $50,000 (up to $10,000 per year). Applicants are selected based on academic achievement and must submit a 500-word application statement. The application deadlines are March 15, April 15, June 12, October 15, and November 15, 2025.
