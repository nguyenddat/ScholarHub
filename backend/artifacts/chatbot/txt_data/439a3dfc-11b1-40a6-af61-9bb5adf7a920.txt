439a3dfc-11b1-40a6-af61-9bb5adf7a920: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a fully funded scholarship offered by the University of Cambridge in the UK, open to all international students. It supports PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, Engineering, and Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. Applicants must submit a research proposal aligned with department goals and have experience with large-scale software projects. Eligibility requires a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, and at least one peer-reviewed publication. The application deadline is November 1, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
