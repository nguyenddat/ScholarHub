This opportunity is available in the UK and is open to students pursuing a PhD in Engineering, Applied, and Pure Sciences. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects and submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, and GRE scores may be needed for some candidates. The application deadline is September 27, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
