9c6f1b77-d57b-4e19-9dec-ca0f22378725: The Gorilla International Scholarship is a merit-based and need-based award provided by Pittsburg State University in the United States. It is available for both undergraduate and graduate students across all majors, offering funding between $2,000 and $6,000 applied towards tuition. The scholarship can be renewed for up to four years and is open to all international students, including freshmen, transfer students, and graduate students. Selection criteria include GPA, professional experience, research, English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS/PTE), and other achievements.
