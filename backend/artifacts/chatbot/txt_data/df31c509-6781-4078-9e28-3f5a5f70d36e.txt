df31c509-6781-4078-9e28-3f5a5f70d36e: The George Harris Scholarship in Chemistry Honours is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the School of Chemistry at the University of Sydney, Australia. It provides funding of $8,500 per annum for up to one year to support students in the Honours program. Established in 2023 through a donation from George Harris, the scholarship aims to encourage new discoveries in Chemistry. Applicants must be Australian citizens or permanent residents. The application deadline is February 9, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/international/bachelors-honours/general.html.
