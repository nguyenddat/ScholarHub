0eedd0b9-a5e2-4cb7-8096-efd6d39dbdf7: The ARA Group Indigenous Scholarship is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, to support Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students. It provides $20,500 per annum for up to five years for students enrolled in their first semester of an undergraduate degree in science, engineering, or architecture, design and planning. Applicants must be currently enrolled at the University of Sydney. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is 30 September 2024. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/ara-group-indigenous-scholarship1.html#:~:text=A%20scholarship%20to%20support%20undergraduate%20Aboriginal%20and%20Torres,or%20the%20School%20of%20Architecture%2C%20Design%20and%20Planning.
