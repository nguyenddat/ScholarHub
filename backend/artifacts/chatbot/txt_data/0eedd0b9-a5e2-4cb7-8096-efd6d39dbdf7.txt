0eedd0b9-a5e2-4cb7-8096-efd6d39dbdf7: The ARA Group Indigenous Scholarship is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, aimed at supporting Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students. It provides funding of $20,500 per annum for up to 5 years for students enrolled in their first semester of an undergraduate degree in fields such as Science, Engineering, Architecture, Design, and Planning. The application deadline is September 30, 2024. Preference is given to applicants demonstrating financial hardship. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/ara-group-indigenous-scholarship1.html#:~:text=A%20scholarship%20to%20support%20undergraduate%20Aboriginal%20and%20Torres,or%20the%20School%20of%20Architecture%2C%20Design%20and%20Planning.
