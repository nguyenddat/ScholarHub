17: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships, offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom, provide partial funding for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. The scholarship covers tuition fees and includes a living stipend. Eligible candidates must have a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related field, and experience with large-scale software projects is preferred. Applicants are required to submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. The application deadline is August 20, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
