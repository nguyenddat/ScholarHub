29: The University of Glasgow offers a PhD Studentship in Natural Language Processing, available to all international students in the United Kingdom. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Eligible fields of study include Computer Science and Engineering. Applicants must demonstrate a strong academic background in relevant fields, submit a compelling PhD application aligned with departmental research, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). The application deadline is October 20, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
