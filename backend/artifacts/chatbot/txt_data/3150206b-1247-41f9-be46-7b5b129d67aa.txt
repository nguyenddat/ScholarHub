3150206b-1247-41f9-be46-7b5b129d67aa: The University of Edinburgh offers PhD Scholarships in the School of Computer Science for EU students in the United Kingdom. These scholarships are available for PhD studies in Computer Science and Engineering, providing a stipend to support living expenses. Applicants must demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch and make a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research. Academic excellence or innovation is also considered. GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is November 4, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
