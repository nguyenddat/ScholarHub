583d9543-b26c-494b-8124-c1af21d055b3: The **International Scholarship** is a partial scholarship offered by **Loughborough University** in the **United Kingdom** for **undergraduate** students. It provides up to **25% tuition fee reduction** for the first year and is available to both UK and international students across various majors. The application deadline can be found on the official website. Selection criteria include education, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with specific support for musicians and athletes. Musicians can receive up to **500 GBP** for music tuition, while sports talents can get up to **5,000 GBP** per year for training, contingent on achieving a basic international level in their sport.
