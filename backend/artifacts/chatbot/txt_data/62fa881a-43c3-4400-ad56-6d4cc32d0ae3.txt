62fa881a-43c3-4400-ad56-6d4cc32d0ae3: The David Clarke Memorial Scholarship is a sports scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, available for both undergraduate and postgraduate students in Australia. It provides funding of $15,000 to Sydney University rugby players who demonstrate outstanding academic achievement and potential. Applicants must be enrolled full-time and have completed at least 48 credit points in their degree, with a minimum semester average mark (SAM) of 65. There are no specified requirements for work experience, research, or certifications. The application deadline is not specified. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/david-clarke-memorial-scholarship.html.
