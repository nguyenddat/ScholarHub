638e3759-2ebe-4bca-bf3c-852c04b636fb: The ARA Group Indigenous Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship for undergraduate Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students at the University of Sydney, Australia. It provides funding of $20,500 per annum for up to 5 years, specifically for students enrolled in science, engineering, architecture, design, and planning fields. Applicants must be currently enrolled in their first semester of an undergraduate degree at the university. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is September 30, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/ara-group-indigenous-scholarship1.html.
