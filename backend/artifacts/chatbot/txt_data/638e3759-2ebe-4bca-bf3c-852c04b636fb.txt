638e3759-2ebe-4bca-bf3c-852c04b636fb: The ARA Group Indigenous Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship for undergraduate Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students at the University of Sydney, Australia. It offers funding of $20,500 per annum for up to 5 years, specifically for students enrolled in science, engineering, architecture, design, and planning. Applicants must be currently in their first semester of an undergraduate degree at the university. The application deadline is September 30, 2024. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/ara-group-indigenous-scholarship1.html.
