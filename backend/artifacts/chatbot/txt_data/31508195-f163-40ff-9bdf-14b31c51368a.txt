31508195-f163-40ff-9bdf-14b31c51368a: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are offered by the University of Edinburgh in the United Kingdom for PhD studies in Engineering, specifically in Computer Science. This scholarship provides partial funding, covering tuition fees, and is open to international, EU, and UK students. Applicants must have at least one year of research experience, a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research, proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90), and a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. The application deadline is November 2, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
