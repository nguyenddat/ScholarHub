These scholarships cover tuition fees and provide a living stipend, allowing recipients to engage in advanced research in Applied and Pure Sciences, Computer Science, and Engineering. Applicants must submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals and possess a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, and GRE scores may be necessary for some candidates. The application deadline is June 18, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
