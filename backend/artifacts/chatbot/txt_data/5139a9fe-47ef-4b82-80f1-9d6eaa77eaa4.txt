5139a9fe-47ef-4b82-80f1-9d6eaa77eaa4: The Global Master's Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Birmingham in the United Kingdom. It is available to international students pursuing a Master's degree in various fields, including chemical engineering, civil engineering, mechanical engineering, or physics and astronomy. The scholarship provides funding of up to 2,000 GBP to support students who have demonstrated academic excellence and may face financial challenges. The scholarship is open to students from all regions globally. Specific selection criteria include education, personal attributes, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with varying weights assigned to each.
