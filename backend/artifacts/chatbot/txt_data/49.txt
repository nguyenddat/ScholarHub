49: The University of Glasgow offers a partial funding scholarship for PhD studies in Computer Science, covering tuition fees. This opportunity is available to students from the UK, EU, and all international regions. Applicants must have experience with large-scale software projects, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred. The application deadline is August 4, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
