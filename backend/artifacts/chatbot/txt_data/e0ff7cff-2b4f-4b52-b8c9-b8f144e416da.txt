This scholarship is for a PhD in Applied and Pure Sciences, specifically in Computer Science, and provides partial funding covering tuition fees. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required, along with proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). The scholarship supports full-time study and includes a living stipend. The application deadline is July 8, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
