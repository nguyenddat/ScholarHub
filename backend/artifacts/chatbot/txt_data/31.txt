31: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a fully funded PhD opportunity offered by University College London in the UK, open to all international students. It covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend for studies in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch), and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is August 15, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
