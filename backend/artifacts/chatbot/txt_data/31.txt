31: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a partial funding opportunity for PhD students at University College London, UK, covering tuition fees. It is open to all international applicants in the fields of Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. The application deadline is August 4, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, experience with large-scale software projects, a research proposal aligned with department goals, potential GRE score requirements, and a first-class or upper second-class honours degree.
