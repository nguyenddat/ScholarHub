2b1ea135-c3d3-4105-8f1b-0cc080f4e697: The JW and BK Elkins Architectural Award is a merit-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Sydney School of Architecture Design and Planning. Valued at $1500, it is available to honours level students who have completed the Bachelor of Design in Architecture and are continuing into the honours year. Applicants must have an unconditional offer for the Bachelor of Design in Architecture (Honours) degree. The selection criteria include academic merit and a research proposal. The application deadline is 09 February 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/jw-bk-elkins-architectural-scholarship.html.
