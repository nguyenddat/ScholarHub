33: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a partial scholarship offered by University College London for international students, including those from the UK and EU. It covers tuition fees for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants should have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards are considered advantageous. The application deadline is September 10, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
