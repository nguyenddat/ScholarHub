30: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a PhD opportunity offered by the University of Southampton in the UK, focusing on Applied and Pure Sciences. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. Applicants should have knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and an interest in interdisciplinary projects is preferred. Proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90) is required, along with a previous research award or distinction being advantageous. The application deadline is September 9, 2025.
