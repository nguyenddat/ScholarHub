30: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is a partial funding opportunity provided by the University of Cambridge in the UK for international students pursuing a PhD in Computer Science. The scholarship covers tuition fees and offers a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is September 27, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
