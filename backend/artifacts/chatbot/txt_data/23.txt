23: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity provided by Imperial College London for international students, including those from the EU and the UK. It covers tuition fees and living expenses for PhD studies in Computer Science and Engineering. Applicants must have a strong academic background in relevant fields and submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and a first-class or upper second-class honours degree is preferred. The application deadline is July 21, 2025.
