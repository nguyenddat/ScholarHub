23: The University of Leeds offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to all international students, including those from the EU and the UK. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and demonstrate English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Academic excellence or innovation is also considered. The application deadline is June 20, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
