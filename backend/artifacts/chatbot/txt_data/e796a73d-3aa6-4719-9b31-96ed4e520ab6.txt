e796a73d-3aa6-4719-9b31-96ed4e520ab6: The Bernard Lee Scholarship for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Students is an undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney in Australia. It provides financial support of $8,500 per annum for up to 5 years to eligible Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander students. The scholarship is open to all majors and is available for undergraduate, postgraduate coursework, and postgraduate research degrees. Applicants must have a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50 and must be either enrolled or have an unconditional offer of admission at the University of Sydney. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is 30 September 2024.
