e796a73d-3aa6-4719-9b31-96ed4e520ab6: The Bernard Lee Scholarship for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Students is offered by the University of Sydney to support Indigenous students in Australia. It is available for undergraduate, postgraduate coursework, and postgraduate research degrees across all majors. The scholarship provides $8,500 annually for up to five years. Applicants must have an unconditional offer or be enrolled in an undergraduate degree at the University of Sydney with a minimum 50 Semester Average Mark (SAM). Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. The application deadline is September 30, 2024.
