79a19ee9-f697-4da7-86ec-6ea9b094e154: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity provided by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom, specifically for EU students. It covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend for PhD studies in Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary projects. GRE scores may be required for some, and candidates should have at least one peer-reviewed publication. The application deadline is September 15, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
