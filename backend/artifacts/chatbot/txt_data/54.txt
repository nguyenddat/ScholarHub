54: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship is a fully funded opportunity for international students to pursue a PhD in Engineering at the University of Oxford, UK. The scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals, and demonstrate English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Additionally, having at least one published peer-reviewed paper is preferred. The application deadline is October 8, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
