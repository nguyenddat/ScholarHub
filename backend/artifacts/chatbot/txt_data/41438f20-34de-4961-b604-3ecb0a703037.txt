41438f20-34de-4961-b604-3ecb0a703037: The Azerbaijan Government Scholarship 2025/26 is a fully funded scholarship provided by the Government of Azerbaijan for international students. It covers tuition fees, a monthly stipend, medical insurance, travel costs, and accommodation. The scholarship is available for various degree levels including preparatory courses, undergraduate, graduate, doctoral, and general medicine programs. There are 100 positions available, and applicants can choose any major. The application deadline is May 15, 2025. Selection criteria focus on educational background, with no specific requirements for experience, research, certification, or achievements.
