41438f20-34de-4961-b604-3ecb0a703037: The Azerbaijan Government Scholarship 2025/26 is a fully funded scholarship provided by the Government of Azerbaijan for international students. It covers tuition fees, a monthly stipend, medical insurance, travel costs, and accommodation. The scholarship is available for various degree levels including preparatory courses, undergraduate, graduate, doctoral, and general medicine programs. A total of 100 positions are offered. The application deadline is May 15, 2025. Eligible majors include all fields of study, and there are no specified experience or research requirements.
