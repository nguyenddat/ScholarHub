2aa007bf-d251-4b86-ad1d-d6b136f56edb: The International Scholarship offered by Loughborough University in the United Kingdom is a partial scholarship for undergraduate students from various majors. It provides up to a 25% reduction in tuition fees for the first year. The scholarship is available to international students globally, who are automatically considered upon application. Specific selection criteria include education, personal attributes, experience, research, certification, and achievements, with special funding available for musicians and sports talents. Musicians can receive up to 500 GBP for music tuition, while sports talents may receive up to 5,000 GBP annually for training if they achieve at least a basic international level in their sport.
