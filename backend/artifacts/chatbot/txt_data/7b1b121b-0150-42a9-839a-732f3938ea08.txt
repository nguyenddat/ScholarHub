7b1b121b-0150-42a9-839a-732f3938ea08: The Dialysis Australia Nursing Scholarship is a merit-based undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney's Susan Wakil School of Nursing and Midwifery in Australia. It provides funding of $32,500 per annum for up to three years to students demonstrating financial need or social disadvantage. Applicants must have an unconditional offer of admission or be enrolled full-time in the nursing program and maintain a minimum Semester Average Mark of 50. A personal statement, evidence of financial need, and participation in an interview are required. The application deadlines vary for domestic and international students, with the first deadline for domestic applicants on October 23, 2024, and for international applicants on September 13, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/c/dialysis_australia_nursing_scholarship.html.
