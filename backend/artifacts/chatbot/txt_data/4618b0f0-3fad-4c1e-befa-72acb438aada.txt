4618b0f0-3fad-4c1e-befa-72acb438aada: The University of Birmingham offers fully funded PhD scholarships in Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a bursary. These scholarships are available to all international students, including those from the EU and UK, pursuing degrees in Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. All accepted PhD applications will automatically be considered for these scholarships. Selection criteria include full-time study, relevant experience, a strong PhD application aligned with departmental research, and additional certifications or achievements. For more details, please refer to the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships. The application deadline is available on the original URL.
