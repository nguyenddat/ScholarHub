It covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. The scholarship is open to applicants from the UK, EU, and internationally. Candidates must apply by October 30, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, at least one year of research experience, a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research, and possibly GRE scores. Academic excellence or innovation is also considered. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
