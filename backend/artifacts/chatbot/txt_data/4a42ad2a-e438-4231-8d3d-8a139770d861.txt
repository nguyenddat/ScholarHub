4a42ad2a-e438-4231-8d3d-8a139770d861: The Gorilla International Scholarship, offered by Pittsburg State University in the United States, is a merit-based and need-based scholarship available to international students. It applies to all majors and is open to both undergraduate and graduate students. The scholarship provides a tuition reduction ranging from $2,000 to $6,000 and can be renewed for up to four years. Eligible applicants include new students, transfer students, and graduate students from any country. Selection criteria include academic performance, personal academic activities, professional experience, English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS/PTE), and GRE/GMAT scores. The application deadline and further details can be found on the official website.
