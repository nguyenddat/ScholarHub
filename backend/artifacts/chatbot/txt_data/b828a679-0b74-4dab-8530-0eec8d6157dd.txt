It is available to new undergraduate and postgraduate students from around the world. The scholarship provides a £3,000 reduction in tuition fees for students who are self-funding their international tuition fees of £12,500 or more. The scholarship is applicable to any field of study, and there is no specified application deadline. Selection criteria include unspecified weights for education, personal attributes, experience, research, certification, and achievements.
