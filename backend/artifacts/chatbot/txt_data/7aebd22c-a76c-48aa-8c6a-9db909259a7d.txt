7aebd22c-a76c-48aa-8c6a-9db909259a7d: The Architecture, Design and Planning Dean's International Merit Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for international students pursuing undergraduate degrees in Architecture and Design. Located in Australia, this scholarship provides a fixed amount of $1000. Eligible majors include Bachelor of Architecture and Environments, Bachelor of Design in Architecture, and related programs. Applicants must have an ATAR of 95 or equivalent and should not have completed any prior tertiary studies. The scholarship is awarded based on academic merit as demonstrated through the HSC equivalent. There is no specified application deadline. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/content/corporate/scholarships/a/deans-international-merit-scholarship.html.
