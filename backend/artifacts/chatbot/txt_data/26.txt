26: The PhD in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing scholarship at University College London offers partial funding for tuition to international students, including those from the EU and the UK. This opportunity is available for candidates pursuing a PhD in Computer Science, Engineering, or related fields. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related discipline, demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. The application deadline is November 12, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
