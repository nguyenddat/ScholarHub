f32af1be-803d-4463-929f-c5318c58e4d1: The Cerebral Palsy Alliance Professor Henry J Cowan Memorial Scholarship is a general scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for allied health students. It is available to Australian Citizens, New Zealand Citizens, or Australian Permanent Residents enrolled in undergraduate, postgraduate coursework, or postgraduate research programs. The scholarship provides funding of $1500 and has a deadline of 30 September 2024. Applicants must have a minimum Semester Average Mark (SAM) of 50 and must hold an unconditional offer of admission or be currently enrolled at the University of Sydney. Preference is given to those demonstrating financial hardship. For more details, visit the official link.
