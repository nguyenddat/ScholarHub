aa57f655-b140-40d7-8fa0-1d89324acfa8: The University of Birmingham offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. Open to EU, UK, and international students, these scholarships are available for studies in Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. Applicants must demonstrate experience with large-scale software projects, make a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research, and provide proof of English proficiency (IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 90). Preference is given to those with previous research awards or distinctions. The application deadline is June 23, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
