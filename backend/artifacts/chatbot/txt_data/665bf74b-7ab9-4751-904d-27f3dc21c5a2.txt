665bf74b-7ab9-4751-904d-27f3dc21c5a2: The Douglas International Education Regional Entrance Scholarship is a merit-based scholarship offered by Douglas College in Canada. It is available to both undergraduate and graduate students from around the world. The scholarship provides funding between 1,000 to 2,000 CAD. There is no need to apply separately as students are automatically considered upon application to Douglas College. Eligible candidates typically have above-average academic performance, equivalent to a B grade or higher, and high English proficiency test scores. The scholarship does not specify a deadline, eligible majors, or personal criteria. More details can be found on the official Douglas College website. The scholarship emphasizes academic achievements and English proficiency as key selection criteria.
