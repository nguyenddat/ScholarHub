8721b198-3af0-4e57-9980-8000d5f7f83d: The Judge Ralph J Perdriau Practical Legal Training Scholarship is a legal training scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia. It provides $20,000 in funding for undergraduate and postgraduate students pursuing practical legal training in employment and discrimination law at the Redfern Legal Centre. Applicants must be enrolled in their final semester and hold a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) or Juris Doctor (JD). The scholarship emphasizes a commitment to social justice and the ability to work with disadvantaged individuals. The application deadline is September 29, 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/b/judge-j-ralph-j-perdriau-practical-legal-training-scholarship-fo0.html.
