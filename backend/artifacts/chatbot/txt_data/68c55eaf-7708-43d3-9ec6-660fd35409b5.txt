68c55eaf-7708-43d3-9ec6-660fd35409b5: The University of Cambridge offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. This opportunity is available to students from the EU, UK, and internationally, focusing on Computer Science. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, with a preference for those with experience in large-scale software projects and an interest in interdisciplinary research. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required. The application deadline is July 5, 2025.
