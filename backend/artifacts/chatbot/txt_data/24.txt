24: The Data Science and Machine Learning PhD Studentship is offered by the University of Cambridge in the UK for international students. This scholarship provides a stipend for living expenses but does not cover tuition fees. It is available for PhD candidates in Computer Science and Engineering. Applicants should have a strong academic background in relevant fields, with a preference for those interested in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some applicants, and having at least one peer-reviewed publication is advantageous. The application deadline is October 20, 2025.
