It covers tuition fees for students pursuing degrees in Engineering, Applied and Pure Sciences. Open to UK, EU, and international students, the scholarship requires a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields. Applicants must submit a compelling PhD application relevant to departmental research. GRE scores may be needed for some, and previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is June 27, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
