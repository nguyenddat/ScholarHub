5789e224-2dee-4b5a-802f-4bba5cc53e20: The International Undergraduate Scholarship 2025 is a merit-based scholarship offered by the University of Sheffield in the UK for new international undergraduate students starting in September 2025. The scholarship provides funding of £2,000-£2,500 per year, totaling up to £10,000, applicable to all undergraduate courses except Medicine and Dentistry. Applicants must have a minimum GPA of 60% and at least 120 credits from the previous academic year. The application deadline is June 4, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sheffield.ac.uk/international/fees-and-funding/scholarships/undergraduate/international-undergraduate-scholarship.
