94ac9d94-a1f8-4e17-8ec7-e90e76084cf0: The AI and Robotics Research PhD Scholarship, offered by Imperial College London in the United Kingdom, is a fully funded opportunity for international, EU, and UK students. It covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend for PhD studies in Computer Science and Engineering. Applicants must have a strong academic background in these fields, submit a research proposal aligned with department goals, and may need to provide GRE scores. A first-class or upper second-class honours degree is required. The application deadline is July 21, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
