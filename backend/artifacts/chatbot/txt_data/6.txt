6: The University of Southampton offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Distributed Systems and Cloud Computing for international students in the UK. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Applicants must have a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields and submit a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. GRE scores may be required for some candidates, and previous research awards or distinctions are advantageous. The application deadline is November 4, 2025. For more details, visit the official link.
