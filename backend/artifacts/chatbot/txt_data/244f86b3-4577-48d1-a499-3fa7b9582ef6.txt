244f86b3-4577-48d1-a499-3fa7b9582ef6: The Shanghai Government Scholarships, provided by the Shanghai Municipal Education Commission, are fully or partially funded scholarships available for international students in China. They cover all majors at the Bachelor, Master's, and PhD levels, offering Class A (full), Class B (partial), and Class C (excellence award) funding. The application deadline varies by institution. The scholarship aims to enhance international education in Shanghai. Specific selection criteria regarding education, experience, research, certification, and achievements are not detailed.
