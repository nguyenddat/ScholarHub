f9d4c522-451c-4dc6-a02e-adbf8f30e013: The Chancellor's Award is a $10,000 undergraduate scholarship offered by the University of Sydney for domestic students, including New Zealand citizens, who achieve an ATAR of 99.90 or above in the NSW Higher School Certificate Examination. Eligible applicants must be current school leavers and have a University of Sydney course listed in their UAC application preferences. The scholarship is awarded automatically following the release of ATAR results. There is no specified application deadline. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/chancellors-award.html#:~:text=%2410%2C000%20scholarship%20awarded%20to%20school-leavers%20with%20an%20outstanding,awarded%20automatically%20following%20the%20release%20of%20ATAR%20results.
