64093ad7-c5a1-4b20-8170-efee7409a92f: The Lincoln University Doctoral Scholarships offer a fee waiver and a NZ $30,000 annual stipend for three years to international students pursuing doctoral studies in New Zealand. Sponsored by Lincoln University, this scholarship is open to all fields of study and is based on academic excellence. Applicants must have a minimum GPA of A- (80%) and demonstrate research or relevant experience. The scholarship is available to new doctoral candidates or those who have registered within six months prior to the application deadline of 30 September 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.lincoln.ac.nz/study/scholarships/search-scholarships/lincoln-university-doctoral-scholarships/
