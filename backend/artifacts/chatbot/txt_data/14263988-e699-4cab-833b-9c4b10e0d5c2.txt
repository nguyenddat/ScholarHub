14263988-e699-4cab-833b-9c4b10e0d5c2: The Lincoln University Doctoral Scholarships offer a fee waiver and a NZ $30,000 annual stipend for three years to international students pursuing doctoral studies in New Zealand. Sponsored by Lincoln University, this scholarship is open to all fields of study and is based on academic excellence. Applicants must have a minimum GPA of A- (80%) and demonstrate research or relevant experience. Candidates should be new to the Lincoln University Doctoral program or have registered no more than six months before the application deadline of 30 September 2025. The scholarship is available to all genders globally. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.lincoln.ac.nz/study/scholarships/search-scholarships/lincoln-university-doctoral-scholarships/.
