55: The University of Leeds offers a fully funded PhD scholarship in Computational Biology, available to EU and international students. This scholarship covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend. Eligible fields of study include Engineering and Computer Science. Applicants must demonstrate academic excellence, experience with large-scale software projects, and an interest in interdisciplinary research. GRE scores may be required for some candidates. The application deadline is September 10, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
