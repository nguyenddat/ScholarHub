44b4bcaf-9574-4a7b-a687-b1a587857ffa: The Research Training Program (RTP) International Fee Offset Scholarship is offered by The Australian National University in Australia. It is a full tuition and health insurance scholarship for international students pursuing postgraduate degrees. The scholarship is merit-based, focusing on academic excellence, and is awarded through a competitive ranking process. Eligible candidates must align their research with ANU's strategy and have a qualified supervisor. The application deadlines are 15 April for the year of commencing the HDR Program or 31 August for the year prior. For more details, visit the official link: https://study.anu.edu.au/scholarships/find-scholarship/research-training-program-rtp-international-fee-offset-scholarship.
