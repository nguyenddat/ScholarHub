44b4bcaf-9574-4a7b-a687-b1a587857ffa: The Research Training Program (RTP) International Fee Offset Scholarship is a full tuition and health insurance scholarship offered by The Australian National University in Australia. It is available for all international students pursuing a postgraduate degree (Master or PhD) in any field of study. The scholarship is merit-based, focusing on academic excellence, and candidates are selected through a competitive ranking process. The application deadlines are 15 April in the year of commencing the HDR program or 31 August in the year prior. Additional selection criteria include experience, research alignment with ANU, and other achievements. For more details, visit the official link: https://study.anu.edu.au/scholarships/find-scholarship/research-training-program-rtp-international-fee-offset-scholarship.
