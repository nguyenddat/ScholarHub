98b4c5d0-176c-46ef-94a9-1c7f4863504a: The University of Canterbury Alumni Scholarship is a fee waiver/discount scholarship for both undergraduate and postgraduate students in New Zealand, providing NZ$5,000 towards tuition fees. It is open to all fields of study and requires candidates to have completed or be completing a university entrance qualification. Selection criteria include academic excellence, involvement in extracurricular activities, and alignment with the objectives of the University of Canterbury Alumni South Island Chapter. The application deadline is 15 August. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.hotcourses.vn/study/newzealand/school-college-university/university-of-canterbury/142318/international.html.
