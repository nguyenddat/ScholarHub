769063fe-4907-4a5d-828e-fcfd54aa9eaa: The Autonomous Systems Doctoral Scholarship, offered by University College London, is a partial funding opportunity for PhD studies in the UK. It covers tuition fees for international, EU, and UK students pursuing degrees in Engineering, Applied, and Pure Sciences. The scholarship is aimed at candidates with experience in large-scale software projects and requires a research proposal aligned with departmental goals. Applicants should have a first-class or upper second-class honours degree, and GRE scores may be necessary for some. The application deadline is August 4, 2025. More details can be found at the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
