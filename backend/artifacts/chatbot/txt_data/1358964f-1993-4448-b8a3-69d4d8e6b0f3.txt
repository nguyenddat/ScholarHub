1358964f-1993-4448-b8a3-69d4d8e6b0f3: The University of Birmingham offers fully funded PhD scholarships in the School of Computer Science, covering tuition fees and providing a bursary. These scholarships are available for PhD studies in Computer Science, Applied and Pure Sciences, and Engineering. They are open to applicants from the EU, UK, and all international regions. All accepted PhD applications will be automatically considered for these scholarships. The selection criteria include making a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. The scholarships are open to all genders and mature students. For more details on experience, certification, and achievements requirements, as well as the application deadline, please refer to the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
