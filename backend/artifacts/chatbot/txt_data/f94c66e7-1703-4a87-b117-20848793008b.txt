f94c66e7-1703-4a87-b117-20848793008b: The David Clarke Memorial Scholarship is a sports scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, providing $15,000 in funding. It is available to students pursuing any degree level and major, specifically targeting Sydney University rugby players who demonstrate outstanding academic achievement and potential. Applicants must have a minimum semester average mark (SAM) of 65 and have completed at least 48 credit points in their degree. There are no specified requirements for work experience, research, or certifications. The application deadline is not provided. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/david-clarke-memorial-scholarship.html#:~:text=This%20scholarship%20has%20been%20established%20in%202011%2C%20to,for%20higher%20achievements%20in%20the%20sport%20of%20rugby.
