f94c66e7-1703-4a87-b117-20848793008b: The David Clarke Memorial Scholarship is a sports scholarship offered by the University of Sydney, Australia, providing $15,000 in funding. It is available to students pursuing any major at the undergraduate or postgraduate level. Established in 2011, the scholarship aims to support Sydney University rugby players who demonstrate outstanding academic achievement and potential. Applicants must have a minimum semester average mark (SAM) of 65 and have completed at least 48 credit points in their degree. There are no specified requirements for work experience, research, certifications, or specific achievements beyond academic excellence. The application deadline is not provided.
