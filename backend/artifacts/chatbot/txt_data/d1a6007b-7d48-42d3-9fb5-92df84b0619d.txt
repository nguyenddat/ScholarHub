d1a6007b-7d48-42d3-9fb5-92df84b0619d: The Quantum Computing Research Scholarships are fully funded PhD scholarships offered by the University of Manchester in the United Kingdom. These scholarships are open to international students and cover tuition fees and provide a living stipend. They are available for students pursuing PhD studies in Engineering, Computer Science, and Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants must have a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field, and demonstrate knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch. An interest in interdisciplinary projects and a record of academic excellence or innovation are preferred. The application deadline is August 27, 2025. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
