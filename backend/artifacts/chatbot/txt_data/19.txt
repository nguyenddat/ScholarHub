19: The Cybersecurity Excellence PhD Funding is a stipend-only scholarship offered by the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom for international students. It supports PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences and Engineering. The application deadline is October 21, 2025. Selection criteria include a strong academic background in Computer Science or related fields, a relevant PhD application, and potentially GRE scores. Applicants should have a first-class or upper second-class honours degree. For more details, visit the official link: [View details](https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships).
