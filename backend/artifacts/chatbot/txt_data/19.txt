19: The Computational Biology Fully Funded PhD scholarship is offered by the University of Southampton in the UK for international students. This fully funded program covers tuition fees and provides a living stipend for PhD studies in Applied and Pure Sciences. The application deadline is August 2, 2025. Selection criteria include full-time study, knowledge of machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch), a strong PhD application relevant to departmental research, potential GRE score requirements, and at least one published peer-reviewed paper.
