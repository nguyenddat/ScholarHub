1473d09b-4c38-4843-a631-603e48b2215b: The University of Southampton offers fully funded PhD scholarships in Computational Biology, covering tuition fees and providing a living stipend. These scholarships are available to international, EU, and UK students and focus on Applied and Pure Sciences. Applicants should have knowledge of machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch and are encouraged to submit a strong PhD application relevant to the department's research. GRE scores may be required for some, and having at least one peer-reviewed publication is advantageous. The application deadline is August 2, 2025. More details can be found on the official website: https://www.birmingham.ac.uk/funding/postgraduate/school-of-computer-science-phd-scholarships.
