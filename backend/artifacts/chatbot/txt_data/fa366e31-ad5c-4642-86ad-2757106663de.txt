fa366e31-ad5c-4642-86ad-2757106663de: The Học bổng Tôn vinh Thạc sỹ Lên lớp Quốc tế is a merit-based scholarship offered by Lincoln University in New Zealand. It is available to international students pursuing a 180-credit taught master's program in any field. The scholarship provides up to 10,000 NZD towards tuition fees. Applicants must have a GPA of at least 75% or a B+ average in their final year of a bachelor's program. Selection criteria also consider personal attributes, experience, research, certifications, and achievements. The scholarship is open globally, and further details can be found on the official Lincoln University website. The application deadline is not specified.
