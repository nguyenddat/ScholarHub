241a9fc1-c491-43c0-b29e-284e634f4e8d: The Digital Health Internship scholarship, offered by the University of Sydney's Faculty scholarships, is a merit-based opportunity for students enrolled in a coursework degree at the University of Sydney. This scholarship, valued at $9,000, supports a 12-week internship in the field of digital health. Applicants must be currently enrolled and complete the internship as part of their degree requirements. The application deadline is 22 October 2024. For more details, visit the official link: https://www.sydney.edu.au/scholarships/e/digital-health-internship.html.
