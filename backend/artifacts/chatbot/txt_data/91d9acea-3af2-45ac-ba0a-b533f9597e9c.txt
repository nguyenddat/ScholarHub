91d9acea-3af2-45ac-ba0a-b533f9597e9c: The Vice-Chancellor's Academic Excellence Postgraduate Scholarships, offered by Western Sydney University in Australia, provide a 50% tuition fee waiver for international students enrolling full-time in a postgraduate coursework program. The scholarship covers all fields of study and is valid for up to two years. Applicants must demonstrate academic excellence with a minimum GPA of 5.95 on a 7-point scale in their most recent undergraduate degree. The scholarship is not available to Australian or New Zealand citizens or permanent residents. Interested candidates should refer to the official link for application deadlines and additional details: https://www.westernsydney.edu.au/international/applying/fees-and-costs/scholarships/academic-excellence-pg-scholarships.
